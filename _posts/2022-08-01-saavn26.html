We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favourite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

JioSaavn is looking for a passionate and enthusiastic talent acquisition and HR Operations professional to join the HR team. You will be expected to manage internal as well as external stakeholders, support in manpower planning and work closely with HR Business Partners to deliver technical and non-technical hiring requirements. Additionally, you will be required to facilitate employee onboarding by managing new joiners’ documents verification, background verification, etc.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Regularly partner and work with key business stakeholders and respective HRBPs on hiring requirements and recruitment process for tech and non-tech roles
  • Ideate, implement and execute best in-class initiatives for sourcing, screening and interviewing processes to attract top talent to JioSaavn
  • Own and drive end-to-end recruitment process from sourcing up to onboarding process
  • Negotiate and close the offers with candidates as per the approved budgets, approvals and benchmarks with HR Business Partners
  • Keep continuous engagement with the offered candidates till successful onboarding
  • Maintain hiring dashboard and report KPIs like Hiring TAT, Hiring Costs, Source Efficiency, Conversion Ratios etc.
  • Develop and maintain strong pipeline of potential candidates for critical business roles
  • Maintain records of new joiners’ documentation, background verification and other onboarding operations
  • Support team with regular HR initiatives and regular compliance related operations

REQUIREMENTS:

  • 5-8 years of relevant talent acquisition experience in a start-up culture, preferably media organization
  • Hands on experience with HR Operations and basic understanding of labour compliances is preferred
  • Should be confident in handling stakeholders across levels
  • Expertise in operating on all major recruitment channels, like LinkedIn, Naukri, Jazz, etc.
  • Experience in managing business stakeholders and recruitment vendors
  • Fluent English communication skills, both written and oral
  • Proficient in reporting recruitment dashboards, manpower planning, costing and budgeting exercise
  • Must have strong screening, negotiation and closing skills
  • Should be able to deliver effective results within timelines
  • Should collaborate effectively within team and maintain consistent communication
