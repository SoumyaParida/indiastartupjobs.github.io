ROLE:

JioSaavn is seeking a Manager Data Analyst to join our growing global team of entrepreneurs. This role will be an integral part of the Business Insights team and will serve as the expert from an analytics perspective for a specific domain area. The Business Insights team is responsible for providing data driven insights and analytics support to all departments across the company including, Product, Marketing, Editorial, Podcasts, Strategy & Ops, and Senior Leadership.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Partner with stakeholders across departments as the analytics expert for that domain area
  • Proactively identify ways in which complex and often ambiguous business problems can be solved systematically with the help of data
  • Run complex queries in SQL and manipulate data using Numpy/ Pandas to support ongoing project needs
  • Create and regularly update data dashboards in Tableau and Google Sheets that identify relevant metrics and monitor associated performance and key trends
  • Work closely with Product and Engineering teams to design and evaluate performance of ongoing experiments
  • Identify pain points that would be best addressed through predictive analytics and build relevant models that are functional and support decision making
  • Provide expertise on new metrics and analysis techniques that push the envelope on current understanding of our users, content, and product offerings
  • Identify opportunities for process improvement and automate repetitive tasks where possible and relevant
  • Create presentations that effectively communicate key insights from rigorous data analysis to technical and non-technical audiences
  • Build strong relationships with stakeholders across departments and proactively anticipate areas that may require analytics support

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s or higher degree in Data Analytics, Data Science, Statistics, Math, Engineering, Computer Science, or other related quantitative fields
  • 5-8 years of experience in an analytical role working with large and complex data sets to provide insights
  • 5-8 years of experience working at an advanced SQL level to query, manipulate, and generate data sets
  • 5-8 years of experience manipulating data in Numpy/Pandas and other data centric ibraries in Python (or similar experience in R)
  • 5-8 years of experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau and/or Python libraries such as Matplotlib
  • Knowledgeable on best practices in experiment design and evaluation
  • Experience building predictive models to solve business problems
  • Experience with writing scripts to automate reporting and other repetitive tasks
  • Advanced Excel (or Google Sheets) and Powerpoint (or Keynote) user
  • Passion and deep understanding for the technology, media, and entertainment space
