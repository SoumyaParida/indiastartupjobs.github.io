Who we are:

Vivriti is a young, dynamic, and spirited institution that boasts of a large appetite for success through its vision of creating the largest and most valuable platform for enterprise finance. The word ‘Vivriti’ means progress or development and speaks volumes about our passion to continuously strive for results that add value to our ecosystem. At Vivriti, we are mission driven, challenging the status quo each day, reinventing the Indian mid-market debt market space, and creating an industry in itself.

Over the last five years, the group that consists of two companies – Vivriti Capital, a unique enterprise fintech NBFC, and Vivriti Asset Management, India’s fastest growing digital alternate debt fund manager, has built significantly on its vision. Vivriti presently manages assets in excess of US$ 800 Mn, has offices in Chennai, Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi and Jaipur, and consists of a high-powered team of 170+.

About the team and role

Lending Team has been tasked with ensuring steady revenue stream for the company through sourcing of Debt business across diversified client base while maintaining excellent client experience. Role will entail leading business across a particular Geography and interacting with various clients at CXO level for providing debt solutions through structured financial products for on balance sheet exposure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Responsible for deal origination for existing as well as new clients and understanding the client’s debt requirement.
  • Utilize all possible channels to source quality clients with potential to generate business across different products
  • Responsible for the process of new client acquisition from identifying prospects, early engagement to facilitating in the deal closure
  • Initiate and manage client engagement by building relationships at CXO level.
  • Devise and implement growth strategies to grow the client base within specific geography.
  • Work closely with the Client Solutions team, Risk, legal and other internal stakeholders to identify solutions and provide smooth experience.

Desired Skills and Experience

  • MBA Finance / CA
  • Possess 6 - 10 years of relevant experience in wholesale / B2B lending across sectors i.e., structured finance products, mid-market corporate lending preferably with an NBFC.
  • Be brilliant with numbers, analysing financial statements, interpreting data and solving problems and at the same time be able to build business relationships, talk to various levels of an organisation (CXOs to analysts), get work done by various people.
  • Ability to structure, discuss the deal contours with the relevant stakeholders – internal and external, and execute the transactions to meet the client's complex financing requirements (end to end) within the purview of business and regulatory requirements.
  • Be personable and have a presence; and be able to hold conversations and negotiations.
  • Present / articulate / position an idea compellingly with strong oral and written communication skills.
  • Make sense of the macros –economic analysis, sectoral drivers, what regulators do.
  • Possess a good eye for detail and ability to work effectively under pressure
  • Think strategically – convey as much in stakeholder conversations and also aid in thought leadership within the team
  • Ambitious and Ability to work in a fast-paced dynamic environment
