Department

Product Management

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Program manage the testing, design, and drawing package creation for new robotic system product lines
  • Build program timelines, milestones, priorities, budget, and personnel
  • Align stakeholders to maintain/update the program plan through regular meetings and off-cycle communication with stakeholders from manufacturing, operations, product, and engineering
  • Track progress (actual vs estimated time/cost/headcount) and report regularly (weekly/monthly/quarterly) to key stakeholders
  • Build technical requirements docs to help engineers consume product specifications
  • Implement regular technical design reviews to help transfer designs to manufacturing
  • Define objectives and metrics for programs with stage gates

Requirements:

  • Fluency in English and proven record of high-quality technical documentation.
  • Proven track record of successfully launching B2B technology products and services with geographically distributed teams in multi-vendor environments.
  • Strong project management skills with the ability to work on and track multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Working knowledge in at least one development Management tool (JIRA, WRIKE etc)

Good to Have:

  • Experience with Scrum and/or past experience as a Scrum master
  • Specific program / project management experience in the development phase of robotic or electromechanical products
  • Project Management Professional (PMP) certification or equivalent.
  • Experience in problem solving within fast-paced and constantly changing environments.
  • Experience in communicating technical concepts to non-technical audiences
  • Experience working cross-functionally in a highly integrated team composed of both technical and non-technical members.
  • Strong spoken and written communication skills.

Eligible candidates:

  • Desired Qualification: B. Tech in Computer Science/Robotics/Mechatronics or a related field. Masters in Robotics and Automation Systems is a plus
  • 5+ years of experience with project management tools
  • 4+ years of software development experience(programmer) in any of robotics, DevOps tech-digital transformation

We Value:

  • Constant learner to become a Subject Matter Expert
  • Proven record in start-up environment working with innovations
  • Exposure to a high paced working environment
  • Ability to conduct detailed procedures in a time-constrained environment
