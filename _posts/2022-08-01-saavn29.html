ROLE:

JioSaavn is looking for a few candidates towards the "Associate Lyrics & Annotations" towards the Music Research Team. We are looking at candidates for the following languages:

  • Telugu

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Annotate/Explain Hindi/Telugu/Tamil lyrics on a daily basis
  • Translation and transliteration of Hindi/Telugu/Tamil lyrics in English
  • Generate interesting trivia related to the music
  • Transcribe lyrics as well as correct them wherever applicable
  • Proofread to make sure content generated is accurate and appropriate
  • Create weekly list of songs to annotate
  • Escalate issues to the team manager when appropriate
  • Assist team manager and JioSaavn management with special projects when necessary
  • Maintain a professional working relationship with colleagues
  • Work effectively to achieve individual, team and company objectives
  • Provide flexible support for team members and other JioSaavn teams and foster a positive and motivating environment

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be adept at both reading and writing in that specific language (Telugu/Tamil/Hindi etc)
  • Must be deeply aware of the music and artists in that specific language (both mainstream and independent music)
  • Must be equally adept at articulating in English (writing)
  • Good internet research skills.
