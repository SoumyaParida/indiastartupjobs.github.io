About the company :

For the uninitiated, Treebo is a new age hotel brand offering comfortable stays at budget prices. Staying at budget hotels has always meant making compromises on quality. Well, not anymore. At Treebo, we are putting dignity back into budget travel with our unmatched 100% Quality Guarantee. Founded in 2015, we currently operate more than 400 properties across 80 locations in India. We are hell bent on changing the way customer experience is delivered in the industry. So if you are passionate about being part of a truly game-changing team, look no more. This might just be the perfect opportunity for you!!

Roles and Responsibilities:

As a Head of Product Management, you will be a member of our leadership team. You will be charged with building and leading a product organization, generating a talent pipeline, hiring talented, high performing product managers and setting up systems to grow talent. You will model our company values of being humble, mission driven, resilient, and creative when solving complex challenges while driving those values across the organization.

You will actively engage with executive leadership, participate in the product discussion, and handle sensitive escalations.

What You’ll Do:

  • Spearhead a world-class team of Product managers and define the long-term strategy and vision for the product, leveraging research, experience and new technologies to deliver features to customers/stakeholders
  • Bring value addition by thinking big and continuously pitching new product ideas
  • Drive requirement definition, customer experience design, product roadmap and prioritization
  • Define and own the business metrics. Work with the Product Leadership to prioritize problems or themes to reach the outcomes.
  • Ability to translate complex processes into technical requirements while also managing delivery and operations
  • Work with the Engineering and design teams to build a product that meets the expected outcomes and market needs

What You’ll Need:

  • 10+ years of experience, demonstrating management of tasks, resolution of problems and issues and experience in implementing action plans to meet objectives. 8+ years of product management experience and an academic background of Post graduation in Management (MBA) or equivalent
  • Define strategy and vision for the Business you will lead. Experience leading product teams establishing product roadmaps and ensuring teams deliver on key achievements; with an India/Global customer base.
  • Excellent communicator who can deal with the most ambiguous situation and be able to present data to be able to operate.
  • An obsession with technology - enthusiastic in developing understanding the potential of technology and how to convert it to solve innate consumer needs
  • Knowledge in developing elegant and simple feature functionalities which deliver the world-class user experience
