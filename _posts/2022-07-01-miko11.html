About Miko 3

Miko 3 is designed as a smart companion robot for children in the age group 5- 10 years old. Miko’s got a personality, dozens of emotions and smart learning AI to engage children in broad fun and learning experiences. Not just recognizing you and calling you by name, it tells you stories, rhymes and can show quick dance moves.

Miko’s platform team looks into the overall robot-child interaction like exploring and defining relevant use-case scenarios to deliver meaningful and playful experiences. We are a multi-disciplinary team of Design Researchers, Product designers, Content writers, Product managers and Engineers.

Relevant experience:

  • Minimum 4-6 years of Product design/ UX/ Interaction design experience in a design studio or tech startup.
  • Bachelor’s and/or graduate degree in HCI, Interaction design, New Media, Graphic design or other relevant degree.
  • Strong portfolio and a range of experiences in multiple fields within product design like collaborating with product managers and developers in understanding business goals and creating design requirements, conducting user research, creating and testing wireframes and prototypes with target personas, shipping high fidelity UI designs to developers
  • Relevant experience with a variety of design and prototyping tools (e.g. Figma, Sketch, Framer, Principal app, Adobe suite etc).
  • You work best in a participatory, team-based environment. You support and inspire teams through collaboration as well as direction, vision, and planning of deliverables.
  • Key responsibilities
  • Work closely with product managers, developers and other creatives to bring organization’s business and product goals to life
  • Get exposure to and involvement with all aspects of the design: research, inspiration gathering, brainstorming, strategy defining, concept development, prototyping etc.
  • Create, scale and maintain consistency of various UI elements in the Design Component system
  • Support and mentor junior design talent across the team
