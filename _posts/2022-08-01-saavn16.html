ROLE:

We are looking for a smart and motivated individual to play a key role in supporting the Music Publishing at JioSaavn. This role will involve working across the Content, Legal and Finance teams to collect and analyze relevant data, track the status of various music publishing licensing agreements, develop reports for stakeholders, perform general tasks such as calendaring, filing and budget tracking, and coordinate various administrative matters directly with the business group.

The candidate also needs to be client-service oriented, give great attention to detail and have a high level of professionalism, integrity, and discretion in handling confidential information.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Work on existing and new JioSaavn licenses, establish and maintain key relationships with the important rights holders and help in closing publishing licenses.
  • Work with and support the internal teams on Legal, Licensing and internal financial controls.
  • Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders on strategic music publishing projects and advise them on identifying licensing gaps and needs.
  • Support the publishing team in negotiating deals, including the securing of public performance and compulsory licenses for the interactive and non-interactive streaming of underlying musical works on the JioSaavn platform.
  • Duties also include the preparation of relevant historical streaming data and revenue calculations and the review and analysis of licensing agreements.
  • Work on reporting and payment requirements related to publishing, licensing and royalty accounting.
  • Work strategically with the other internal business teams to ensure that the publishing licenses reflect and accommodate JioSaavn’s evolving business needs.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • 3-5 years of experience in working in the music industry preferably in a similar role.
  • Basic knowledge of music publishing and licensing matters.
  • Understanding of music copyright and challenges and opportunities the industry faces today.
  • General knowledge of the digital music landscape, streaming services and industry best practices.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Microsoft Excel, Word and PowerPoint.
  • Ability to plan and deliver on tight deadlines with attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal skills.
  • A love for music, technology and an insatiable desire to learn.
