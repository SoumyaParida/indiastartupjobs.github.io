Description

Founded and headquartered in Bangalore, India, UrbanPiper is a leading SaaS platform in the restaurant industry. We work with eateries of all sizes - from your local takeaway to global restaurant chains across the world to grow their in-store and online business with intelligible software & our out of the box solutions. From automating workflows across online delivery platforms such as Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Just Eat and more — to building and deploying self-branded websites and apps, UrbanPiper is the preferred choice for over 20k+ restaurants across the globe.

We are backed by top VCs — Tiger Global and Sequoia Capital. Recently, we closed our Series B round of funding with Swiggy and Zomato also participating together! That’s a first and only (until now) event for any startup in the food and beverage industry.

About the role

We are looking for a talented Field Marketing Manager, International Expansion to join our team and help power the next phase of UrbanPiper’s growth! This is an opportunity for someone who doesn’t shy away from team collaboration and project management. In this role, you will become an audience, industry, and product expert in order to develop the field marketing strategy and campaigns that help power UrbanPiper’s International Expansion goals. You will be responsible for all ATL & BTL activities that help build UrbanPiper’s for a set of international markets.

Our ideal candidate will have experience running field marketing campaigns by utilising a number of different channels in the B2B tech space. You’ll be able to influence our overall marketing strategy as well as roll up your sleeves to execute on it. You will be comfortable working cross-functionally with teams like sales, and product. You are thoughtful and creative, with a strong bias to action and a refusal to settle for good enough. Ultimately, you should be able to oversee all brand marketing initiatives to build brand awareness and increase brand consideration in the international markets you are responsible for.

  • Work closely with Channel Partners to create effective co-marketing campaigns and execute them seamlessly

  • Create and manage a partner network of key stakeholders and influencers to grow UrbanPiper’s brand through a mix of marketing activities
