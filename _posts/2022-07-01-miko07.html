We are looking for a candidate who is hands-on when it comes to working with or creating complex electronic systems. This includes being familiar with both HW and FW.

As a Sr. System Engineer, the successful candidate will be part of the systems engineering team that is responsible for defining, integrating, and testing complex electronic systems.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Participate in system design, development, verification, troubleshooting, and delivery of a system or subsystems.
  • Develop project specifications and release documentation and/or all phases of the project development cycle from definition and design, through implementation including Architecture/ Design.
  • Perform Engineering/Trade Analysis, problem identification, and troubleshooting of complex system-level issues with no supervision.
  • Reproduce Field failures to identify the root cause for the issues, and work with cross-functional teams to find and deploy solutions for the issues.
  • Identify continuous improvement in the design to improve customer experience and reliability of the system.
  • Requirement management and documentation of system behavior
  • Responsible for the NPD, sustenance, and enhancement of hardware concepts.
  • Assist in the coordination of various teams testing and evaluating for the development of design and its implementation of the best output.
  • Guide the team for developing test automation setups that can evaluate the system behavior and allow multiple tests to be run sequentially automatically.
  • Mentor other engineers throughout the company and seek to continually improve processes companywide.

REQUIRED SKILLS:

  • Bachelors in Engineering with 5+ years of experience.
  • Hardware-Firmware co-design.
  • Hands-on experience with electrical and electronics systems.
  • Good understanding of fundamental EE engineering concepts, including circuit theory, and embedded systems.
  • Highly developed hardware design skills with extensive knowledge of IoT architectures.
  • Good communication skills.
  • Analytical and decision-making skills.
  • Ability to work effectively within a cross-functional team with ambiguous requirements.
  • Should know how to code MCU and how cloud system works.
  • Should have a creative and curious way of working leading to continuous improvements.
  • Must be open-minded and willing to accept new challenges.
  • Strong written and oral communication skills.
