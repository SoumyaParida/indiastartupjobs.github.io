Job Description:

Talent Acquisition-:

  • Identifying future hiring needs and developing job descriptions and specifications
  • Collaborate with functional heads to compile a consistent list of requirements
  • Attracting suitable candidates through databases, online employment forums, social media, etc.
  • Conducting interviews and sorting through applicants to fill open positions
  • Completing paperwork for new hires
  • Promoting the company’s reputation and attractiveness as a good employment opportunity

Learning & Development-:

  • Writing training content and building an e-learning content.
  • Understand complex topics and represent them in a simple and engaging format
  • Develop assessment and learning activities
  • Create media to support learning- audio visual aids

Alternate Supply Model (ASM)-

  • Connect with Village Panchayat’s / influencers and promote our job opportunities in identified districts(Manpower Gold Pots) for Bikers ( Electric Vehicles) across the country. Also for other openings.
  • Do develop sustainable relationships with Panchayats.
  • Provide backend support for Job Fairs and attend them as and when required
  • Identify areas for job fairs based on requirements
  • Call short listed candidates and engage them till they join and complete training
  • Identify villages from where we get maximum joiners and plan Village CSR activities
  • Maintain and provide dynamic MIS and dashboards
  • Any other related tasks that may be assigned from time to time

Essential/ likely experience--

  • Proactive and initiative, with sound problem solving skills and hands-on approach
  • Strong decision making and problem-solving skills.
  • Stakeholder Management
