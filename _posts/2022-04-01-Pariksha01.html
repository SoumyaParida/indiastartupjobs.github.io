Pariksha is India's largest vernacular test preparation App that helps aspirants from Bharat prepare for any state government job exam in the language of their choice, through videos and live streaming classes in the most affordable, convenient, and enriching manner. We are currently operational in 13 states and 8 languages across India with a constant zeal to expand further. Pariksha is founded by an alumnus of IIT Kanpur and Symbiosis International.

Our Mission is to make affordable, outcome-based, top-notch test preparation available to all by democratizing equal access to opportunity by creating a level playing field in test preparations. We want to touch and enrich lives one at a time by providing easy access to desired content and test prep in every part of India, and the World!
