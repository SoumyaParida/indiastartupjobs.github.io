About PropVR

PropVR is a wholly owned subsidiary of Squareyards, working on cutting edge technologies such as AI, VR and AR. With over 5 international patents, the company is disrupting 3D visualization for real estate, and building the future portfolio of AI/ML driven solutions.

Square Yards is India's largest full-stack proptech platform; playing the entire consumer journey from Search & Discovery, Transactions, Mortgages, Home furnishing, Rentals and Property Management. Square Yards is also building B2B disruptive proprietary platforms for stakeholders such as Developers, Financial Institutions, and Agents. The company has presence in over 9 countries and over 5300+ workforce globally.
