About Pratilipi

Our Engineering team works closely with the Product, Design, Business and Data Science teams to build experiments & features that enable delightful experiences for our Users. Hence we fight a lot and party even more.

We are a lean team that learns from each other and iterates rapidly to make a larger impact. Any point in time, we are seen making (and breaking) high user impact features in production.

Our Micro-services architecture allows us to be Polyglot in the technology stack. We are completely hosted on AWS Platform. We are available to users via Web, Android and iOS.

Our tech team consists of 30+ engineers who work in a high ownership driven environment, from fixing bugs to developing features to organizing stand up comedy shows in the office.

With an organisation strength of 135+, we are looking for accountable engineers who fit into our culture and bring something new to the table, including party tricks.

You will be handling one of the product-engineering team of the Core Product at Pratilipi. You will be responsible for delivering the KPIs along with maintaining the team members learning curve & product quality.

Responsibilities:

  • You will be building users experiences along with the team to solve our User problems
  • You will be working with engineers, product, design, qa and platform to build new user experiences while maintaining the quality of the product
  • You will be working with teams across organization to launch new user experiences at scale
  • You will be empowering the engineering team to achieve a high level of technical productivity and quality
  • You will be helping the team members with their career growth with excellent and continuous feedbacks
  • You will be mentoring team members to achieve excellency
  • You will be hiring diverse engineers in your team while working very closely with the talent acquisition team
  • You will be contributing to engineering-wide initiatives
  • You will be actively involved in strategic direction and product decisions that impacts all our Users

We are looking for someone who has:

  • 7+yrs experience
  • Excellent Communication Skills
  • Some experience in handling engineering team earlier
  • Designed systems that can scale
  • Strong hiring skills
