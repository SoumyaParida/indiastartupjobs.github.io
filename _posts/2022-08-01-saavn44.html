ROLE:

We’re looking for a Devops Lead Engineer to ensure JioSaavn is fast, reliable, and available for tens of millions of users. You will work in a closely-knit team to proactively monitor, automate and improve end-to-end system performance, identify bottlenecks, and potential failures. You will report to the Manager in the engineering team.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Managing and training 2-3 members of team.
  • Managing production systems, devops tools, supporting internal engineering teams.
  • Managing infrastructure and its costs on Azure Clouds.
  • Build tools to reduce occurrences of error and improve ecosystem health.
  • Ensure our platform exceeds goals for availability, capacity, efficiency, scalability, and performance.
  • Diagnose and resolve latent and systemic reliability issues across stack: application and network.
  • Participate in production issue escalations.
  • Proactively monitor and improve end-to-end system performance, identify bottlenecks, and potential failures.
  • Develop and maintain scalable alerting tools for debugging and monitoring.
  • Automate and own all production infrastructure, deployment, upgrades, and patches.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Masters or Bachelors in Computer Science or Computer Engineering or CDAC (or equivalent courses)
  • At least 7 to 10 years of hands on experience with DevOps Engineer.
  • Independently learning and sharing new technologies with teams.
  • Experience managing very large-scale, public-facing websites.
  • Understanding of all the infrastructure pieces and protocols needed to make big sites work.
  • Automating and Orchestrating workloads across multiple public cloud platforms. Automation experience with at least one configuration management system such as Terraform, Ansible, Puppet, AWS Cloud formation or other such tools.
  • Expertise in tooling frameworks such as ELK, Prometheus, and Grafana.
  • Excellent programming (Bash, Python, Go or preferred scripting languages) and Git workflows.
  • Proficient in leveraging CI and CD tools to automate testing and deployment.
  • Experience working in Agile/Scrum/Kanban environment.
  • Knowledge of at least one cloud platform AWS, Azure, GCP.
  • Should have migration experience from cloud to cloud or hardware to cloud etc.
  • Good Docker and Kubernetes experience.
  • Must have some basic knowledge of cloud, application, network and infrastructure security.
  • Cloud, ISO, ISMS, PCI DSS etc audit experience plus.
  • Experience on building setup from scratch plus.
  • Redhat certified and AWS certification plus.
