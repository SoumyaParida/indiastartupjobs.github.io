OUR STORY:

JioSaavn is South Asia’s leading music streaming service. We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favorite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

As a product leader of data products, you will be part of the Ad Tech Product team that handles the core product responsibilities of ad tech automation products and data products for JioSaavn ad platform customers. Your work will help us create innovative, cutting edge, and highly scalable data products, which will contribute to justifying campaign ROI and campaign performance measures for all advertisers coming on board with JioSaavn ads.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Own the product roadmap and drive the product discovery and delivery for Ad data products
  • Create efficient feedback loops and manage internal Ad Tech stakeholders from product planning to launch
  • Work with the Product team to prioritize and decide the data cuts, metrics, visualizations that should go into Intellect or other Ad Platform products
  • Work with the Data team to perform analysis to derive useful insights that can be productised for our stakeholders and customers
  • Be hands-on with creating and evolving product design mockups before handing them over to Tech and Data teams for development

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Excellent soft skills to manage stakeholders’ expectations and handle clear communications across the board
  • Agility in shipping high-quality products and creatively figuring out processes to quickly resolve blockers to productization
  • Prior product experience is a good-to-have
  • A background in tech will be a big plus
  • Working knowledge of advertising technologies will be a big plus
