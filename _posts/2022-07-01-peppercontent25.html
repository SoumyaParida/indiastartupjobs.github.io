Pepper (www.peppercontent.io) is building a global content marketplace that brings companies and content creators together to scale up content creation processes across 75 content verticals and 150+ content categories. Over the past 3.5 years we’ve worked with brands like India Today, Amazon India, Adobe, Swiggy, Dunzo, Businessworld, Times Group, Digit, Wittyfeed, YourStory etc. along with 1000+ brand companies like Amazon, Adobe, Google, CRED, BookMyShow, UpGrad, Yulu, Paisabazaar, Zivame, Yatra, Bewakoof, Imarticus Learning etc. to create content that is meticulous and customized according to our customers’ needs.

We are on the path to building a global on-demand content marketplace platform that allows for collaboration, discovery and upskilling for all our creators. Now we are in a rapid expansion phase to enter into more content service offerings and ensure the quality of deliveries are top notch. To achieve this, we are looking for a Category Operations Lead for our ‘Content Writing’ Category who will be responsible for cross team collaboration and management of content orders for efficient and quality delivery

We’re looking for someone who is enthusiastic, comfortable with uncertainty, flexible, great with people and isn’t afraid to roll up their sleeves and get their hands dirty.

If you think you have the spark and a constantly simmering pot of ideas, this is the chance for you!

Job Responsibilities:

  • Overlooking and managing the entire text content operations for all customers i.e. managing the journey of every content piece at Pepper in sync with the entire text operations team
  • Collaborating with stakeholders such as customer service team, creation team, editorial team and supply team to drive the completion of projects to a success.
  • Responsible for the bottom of the funnel revenue for the text category.
  • Project timeline estimation for potential customers before the start of the project
  • Strategic planning and optimisation of operational processes to reduce the TAT and increase the quality of content.
  • Identifying and removing roadblocks faced by any team in executing any project
  • Standardizing and optimising the text content brief format for each and every vertical in favour of the mutual benefit of both - customers and creators.
  • Strategizing, initializing and executing bulk content projects for resources, operations and scalability
  • Handling revisions of the entire order requested by customers.
  • Monitoring and removing inefficiencies from the system
  • Gathering insights to help in productizing the entire operational flow

The ideal candidate must have:

  • 5-6+ years of experience in a high growth start-up in an execution/ operations first role and preferred experience in supply-chain management
  • Should be able to scale the operational processes by collaborating with multiple high-paced stakeholders
  • Excellent verbal and written communication
  • Attention to detail and data backed approach
  • Should understand basics of content creation in text (can be an avid reader or writer too)
  • Should have all that it takes to succeed in a startup environment flexibility, working beyond the call of duty, ability to multitask and deliver under pressure, and a high level of ownership
