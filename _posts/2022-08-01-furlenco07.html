About Furlenco

Furlenco is a furniture and appliance subscription platform. We enable the urban Indian to live better today in their homes, by providing access to award-winning furniture and home decor on rent. We’re technology driven and leverage technology for everything from customer experience to risk management and fulfilment services.

Job description

Furlenco is looking for an experienced Senior Product Manager to drive our core subscriptions and order fulfilment services. This includes order processing and fulfilment platforms, subscription billing and payments, risk platforms and a collection of supporting services. As a Product Manager, you’ll own the entire product lifecycle from requirements to GTM of your products. You’ll also collaborate with multiple teams and ensure that all systems are working perfectly.

What’ll you do

  • You’ll own multiple products and drive efforts across their lifecycle
  • You’ll collaborate with business teams to understand the requirements and transform the vision and goals into a product roadmap
  • You’ll align with UX, Engg & QA teams to drive execution of product features
  • You’ll track performance of all your products and iteratively work on improving them
  • You will also evangelize your product to all the stakeholders and drive successful adoption

Requirements

  • 4-8+ years as a Product Manager where you’ve owned complete products
  • Good grasp of how microservices work & how to design independent modules that come together to perform a massive task
  • Strong grasp on APIs, data modelling and querying
  • Ability to drive adoption, monitor metrics and iterate with business

Good to have skills:

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, information systems, or a related field is preferred
  • MBA is preferred
  • Experience in building products from scratch and overseeing its evolution
