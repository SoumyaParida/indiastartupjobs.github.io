At smallcase, our mission is to change how India invests. We develop delightful, simple & transparent investment products and experiences for individuals to invest in stocks & ETFs. Over 3 million investors use our apps and products on a monthly basis.

In the last 5 years, we have orchestrated a fast-growing ecosystem of 300+ large, fast-growing and ambitious brands in the capital markets ecosystem by building a unique technology platform & API layer. We are a young, innovative team of 250 headquartered in Bangalore, yet operate in a remote-first way. Smallcase is backed by world-class investors including funds like Sequoia Capital, Blume Ventures, Faering Capital, Premji Invest, institutions like Amazon, HDFC Bank and notable individuals including Hemendra Kothari (DSP), Nithin Kamath (Zerodha), Kunal Shah (CRED), Tanmay Bhat.

Responsibilities include

  • Execute the design projects across the entire product life cycle of the smallcase Publisher platform. You collaborate and contribute to build the vision for the user experience
  • Be meticulous. Be autonomous. You’ll take full ownership of your work, and you take responsibility for every last detail, every step of the way.
  • Work with multi-disciplinary teams as you’ll interact closely with engineering, product, and business folks to find elegant but practical solutions to design challenges.
  • Rapidly produce multiple concepts; knowing when to apply pixel-perfect attention to detail, and when to make low-fi sketches and prototypes.
  • Test the flows, screen implementations, copy/content, to ensure the end customers’ experience is top-notch. We take that very seriously.
  • Ensure all this is done and dusted in tight timeframes and constraints. That’s where we expect the problem solver in you to come up with uber-quick feasible solutions.
  • Staying in the loop and on top of the latest standards, changes, trends in the fields of user-interface, user-experience, information-architecture and more.

Requirements

We are looking for highly motivated designers who thrive in an ultra-high pace work environment. You will fit in so great if you:

  • Have prior experience of 3+ years crafting product experiences for multiple screen sizes & platforms
  • Have an excellent sense of visual (typography, font pairings, color theory & visual hierarchy) and interaction skills (micro-interactions & animations) along with proficiency in UI and interaction design tools - Sketch / Figma / After Effects / Photoshop / etc.
  • Has a stronghold on common UX patterns, UI Patterns for different platforms & form-factors
  • Have a good understanding of prototyping & Information Architecture
