About the team and role

The Head – Data Science is responsible for leading the function including

  • Data Science team
  • Data Engineering team

The Data Science team will work on multiple initiatives including

  • Set up a “Credit Engine” that encompasses onboarding of new clients and monitoring clients with an “Early Warning Signals” system to assess and monitor the entire book. This is a challenging and interesting initiative given the client behaviour will change based on sector/ industry.
  • Create dashboards for visualisation of granular data across client segments,check for seasonality based on client sector and provide specific inputs to Business team for targeting clients
  • Develop and periodically update rating models for each client segment including Enterprises, Financial Services, Supply Chain Finance
  • Run Sensitivity analysis on the book
  • Develop and fine tune models for Expected Losses
  • Forecast the asset book for the company on a monthly and quarterly basis
  • Leverage technology and develop warning indicators with Risk team for highlighting Operational Risks and Fraud Risks

The Data Engineering team will

  • Create and maintain optimal data architecture, assemble large, complex data sets
  • Identify, design, and implement internal process improvements: automating manual processes, re-designing infrastructure for greater scalability
  • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources using SQL and AWS ‘big data’ technologies.
  • Work with stakeholders including Management, Risk, Business, Technology, Data Science teams to assist with data-related technical issues and support their data infrastructure needs.
  • Create data tools for data scientist team members that assist them in building and optimizing our product into an innovative industry leader.

Impact of this Role

This role is of strategic importance to the company as we leverage technology to scale our business and enhance the client value proposition, including

  • Output provided by this team will be used to fine tune our business plans and course correct as needed
  • Enhance productivity of Risk and Credit teams with monitoring and monthly updates on client red flags
  • Enhance scalability of the organisation to grow and manage a large, complex portfolio of clients across industries and across product structures
  • Provide insights to Business teams for targeting specific clients in a sector and timing of approaching clients based on seasonality
  • Adding new ideas and suggestions regarding usage of data science that can provide value to the company.
