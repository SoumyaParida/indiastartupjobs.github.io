About Avataar Reality Labs

At Avataar Reality labs, we are pushing the boundaries of what is possible in AR/VR and enabling the consumer shift to metaverse at scale. We are building the neural engine to empower the creation, rendering and manipulation of AR/XR content across devices. Our projects range from solutions to hard technical problems that significantly boost our current neural rendering engine, to much bolder endeavours that would fundamentally cause a paradigm shift in the way people create and consume content in the mixed-reality 3D world. If thinking about this herculean research and engineering adventure at the intersection of vision, deep learning, and graphics excites you, we can’t wait to have you on board!
