About Yulu

Are you sick of breathing poor quality air in our cities? Do you worry about climate change? Have you felt like pulling your hair on seeing a congested red route on the Google map?

Did you nod in ‘yes’? Same here, we did too! And so, we at Yulu set out to fix all of these issues by making our mobility smart, efficient and sustainable.

Being India’s leading shared electric mobility player with the largest fleet of EVs and biggest Battery-as-a-Service network, Yulu is reimagining urban mobility for a better future. Not only do we provide an affordable, sustainable and accessible commute alternative to daily commuters, but we are also making last-mile goods deliveries cheaper and green. Simply put, we are revolutionizing the way urban India commutes and consumes by building the Mobility 2.0 paradigm.

We are innovating at the intersection of consumer behavior, public policies, urban infrastructure, cutting edge technology, deep learning, product design, supply chain innovation and economic inclusion. Our proprietary mobility platform uses IoT, Machine Learning, and AI for vehicle access, fleet management, intelligent demand-supply management, and efficient operations.

After 3 years of building a strong foundation for electric mobility, now, as we are ready to feverishly scale our business 10x this year and 100x over the next 3 years by working on many new, complex & BIG challenges on technology, data, product, markets, customer segments and growth, we still feel we are just getting started!

If our mission brings a spark in your eyes & if you’d like to join some of the most passionate people in the country who are committed to transform the cacophony on Indian roads into a symphony - Come, join the #Unstoppable Yulu team!!

Role Summary

As Business Head – Delhi NCR, you will be responsible for managing the strategic growth and P&L for the city. Maximizing revenue, deepening customer acquisition, building partnerships while ensuring impeccable operations and asset management will be your focus.

Delhi NCR is divided into multiple smaller clusters and you will be supported by cluster managers, maintenance teams and partnership teams who will report to you. If you are passionate about strategizing Business growth, aligning partnerships and operations towards bringing in that growth, are a self starter and are passionate about sustainable cities, we are looking for you! In short, you will be at the center of making electric mobility a way of life in the Delhi NCR Region while ensuring business growth inline with the long term strategic Vision of YULU.

Key Responsibilities:

Business strategy and growth:

  • Formulate business growth strategy for Delhi-NCR. Formulate growth strategy via different revenue streams– people mobility, goods and enterprise mobility across Delhi – NCR which includes New Delhi, Noida (Gautam Budh Nagar), Gurugram, Faridabad and Ghaziabad.
  • Launch Yulu in new areas and grow existing areas.
  • Deploy plans to ensure maximum profitability with minimum costs. Focus on unit economics to bring about an impact in the city’s P&L
  • Work closely with the marketing team to increase user acquisition within Delhi NCR. Be a brand ambassador of Yulu in the region and be a key representative of the brand in the Delhi NCR region.
  • Understand and analyze demand patterns, strike partnerships that lead to revenue growth.
  • Strengthen relationships with the government bodies, public transportation entities, large real estate entities (residential and commercial) etc.
  • Be aware about the latest developments in the EV/mobility domain and represent Yulu's interest as and where necessary.

Business efficiency and operations:

  • Take ownership of P&L, use metrics -bike availability, revenue, utilization, cost, customer experience to strategize, improve operational efficiency.
  • Establish process control mechanisms for both assets, people, compliances as well as processes. Establish project plans and lead from the front on ground - in line with the five Yulu Operating Principles and the priorities.
  • Encourage and arrive at out of the box solutions for problems. Be an ambassador for creative problem-solving.
  • Work closely with central functions - technology, customer support, operations design, learning and development, finance, marketing and HR to deploy strategic plans along with optimizing efficiency.
  • Track, analyze and report business performance and trends to stakeholders.

Key Requirements:

  • 7 – 10 years of experience. A couple of years heading operations and business strategy/ P&L will be necessary. Experience towards Heading P&L of an area/ region, factory or a plant will be a must.
  • Experience with fleet management, i.e. repairs, maintenance, logistics, supply chain will be an added advantage. 2 - 4 years of experience with business development will be a strong plus.
  • B.Tech from a premier institution. A masters/ MBA will be a plus but is not a key requirement.
  • Understanding of Delhi-NCR at a deep level and preferably a resident.
  • Entrepreneurial and data-driven decision-making approach.
  • A go-getter with an execution-oriented mindset who can set up both, brownfield and greenfield operations.
  • Agility and ability to adapt quickly to changing requirements and scope and priorities.
  • Good understanding of and passion for new age technology concepts.
  • Great communication and interpersonal skills.
