1.1 Job Overview:

  • Designation : Director Collection Strategy
  • Location :Ahmedabad/Bangalore
  • Reporting to : Chief Credit and Risk

1.2 Core Responsibilities:

  • Credit Policy: Formulation & implementation of underwriting strategy and changes in underwriting policy.
  • Preparing and monitoring of Credit Risk Policy for SME segment
  • Creating process notes for the credit underwriting
  • Establishing a robust Credit Analytic Framework and formulating a credit risk process for the lending segment.
  • Interacting with cross functional teams and driving the credit policy agenda and ensuring correct implementation of credit policy guidelines in origination system and decisioning platforms.
  • Analysing the market trends / practices and benchmarking of policies with the market.
  • Underwriting of policy related deviations for the product and proposing policy renewals on a timely basis.
  • Analysis of portfolio and ensuring Portfolio monitoring, trigger reviews & delinquency control.
  • Responsible for establishing proper risk management standards for the portfolio and facilitating the business team in identifying early warning signals and analysing quick mortality cases.
  • Analysing the market trends/practices and benchmarking of policies with the market.
  • Work closely with business, technology, and operations teams to gather the underwriting requirements.
  • Cost Control: Credit Vendor Management.

  • Good industry network to understand the methods being adopted by other NBFCs / banks.
  • Strong organizational, managerial and project management skills
