Job Responsibilities:

This is a high visibility and pivotal role that will work closely with every area of Avataar from our Platform delivery, Engineering to Product and Finance teams.

What you will do:

  • Bring a true passion for building and shipping products and features to make your customers win and drive ROI outcomes.
  • Own end to end delivery process while ensuring best of quality and timelines for all projects.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, develop scope, deliverables, project plans for new initiatives.
  • Help drive revenue growth within accounts through cross-sell and up-sell.
  • Clearly communicate vision, roadmap, and impact for your particular accounts to a spectrum of audiences, from internal stakeholders to the executive team.

Job Requirements

This position requires a candidate with the proven ability to drive process improvements and the ability to keep pace with our explosive growth while motivating others to meet the challenges of an extremely deadline-driven environment.

  • Education: MBA from top-tier B-school.
  • Professional experience: 5+ years of experience in any top tier strategy consulting firm or Start-ups in business operations functions.
  • Communication: Strong executive presence and demonstrated ability to influence and persuade senior leadership. Excellent interpersonal skills and transparent collaborative working style.
  • Superior organizational skills with the ability to juggle many tasks without losing sight of the highest priority items.
  • Hustle: Passion to work in a start-up culture with experience of working in a fast-paced growing team setup and ability to get things done.
  • Drive: Persistence and resilience coupled with story-telling ability and excited to evangelize a new technology painting a vision for clients, partners and internal teams.
  • Humility and quick learner: Down to earth, open to suggestions, investing the time and energy to understand the product and ready to get your hands dirty with whatever the team needs to succeed.
