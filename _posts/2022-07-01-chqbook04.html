Key Skills Required:

  • 5-10 years experience in Collections preferably in NBFCs/BFSI
  • Must be at managerial position - Area Manager/Manager in the last 3 years.
  • Must have managed the team. Must have set processes and policies related to Collections.
  • Exceptional command of verbal and written communication skills
  • MIS/ Analytical skills - must be able to work on dashboards, reports, etc.
  • Strong interpersonal/ conflict management/ negotiation and feedback providing skills

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Create and optimize process for central collections
  • To drive Overall Settlements- Ensure cases settled within the defined TAT and above 95%
  • To ensure negotiation on 100% of cases
  • To negotiate increase in value of the initial offer
  • To drive collections on the agreed settlement value
  • To drive key performance input measures like customer contactability, Login adherence and schedule management
  • Responsible for collection efficiency process within the central team - Improve collection and legal recovery
  • Effective segmentation of the portfolio to maximize recoveries
  • Collaboration with City teams for improvement in daily collections
  • Daily, weekly review to keep a track of team performance against the target. Specifically, track & control the delinquency within various cities, Bucket-wise & DPD wise
  • Review account allocations and collections targets by bucket.
  • Ensure that the collection agencies and executives adhere to the legal guidelines
  • Manage productivity by drawing productivity parameters for collection executives
  • Monitoring performance against set parameters and daily review the same
  • Mentoring and coaching of team
  • Identify and manage training needs for the executives
  • Oversee adoption of Collection Management System within the team.
  • Coordinating and liaising with legal team to ensure legal action is taken on specified cases
  • Liaison with internal and external stakeholders for smooth operations and collections process. Collaborate with the product, Ops, Underwriting and marketing team to identify alternate ways to improve collections
  • Timely maintaining and delivering daily, weekly, monthly dashboard reports.
