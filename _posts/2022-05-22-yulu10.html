Job Summary

The Technical writer will play a pivotal role in creating, developing plans, writing and editing operational, instructional, maintenance, test or user manuals for paper, multimedia or web-based publications. Contributing to the timely design, production and delivery/completion of product documentation and document sets forms the crux of the role.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create and design exceptional documentation in the form of user guides, case studies, market research studies, tech blogs, how-to guides, in-platform instructions and FAQ.
  • Research, write, edit and revise content that is engaging and accessible to user personas with technical and non-technical backgrounds.
  • Work independently to research, investigate, and gather information from formal and informal specifications and requirements sources like Jira epics & stories.
  • Work closely with team members to promote content development best practices, leverage content reuse, promote consistency, and adhere to established styles, conventions, and quality standards.

Key Requirements:

  • Qualification: Bachelor’s degree in English, Communication, Computer Science or related field
  • 2-4 years of experience in technical writing for a SaaS or B2B company
  • Ability to grasp complex technical concepts and communicate the information in a way that is engaging and understood by users.
  • Technical documentation ,Information architecture, Product documentation, XML authoring skill and experience.
  • Proven working experience in technical writing and software documentation.
  • Experience with workplace collaboration tools such as Miro, Jira, Microsoft Teams.
  • Knowledge of new-age content formats, information architecture, and work-flow driven, minimalistic content.
  • Excellent written and oral communications skills.

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
  • A journey that will challenge and reward you in multiple ways.
