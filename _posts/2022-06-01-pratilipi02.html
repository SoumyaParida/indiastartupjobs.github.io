About Pratilipi:

Founded in 2015 by a set of reading enthusiasts, Pratilipi is a storytelling platform which brings readers, writers and their stories together. Something that started as a reading platform we are now operating across three more products - Pratilipi Comics, Pratilipi FM, and IVM podcasts.

All the magic happens from a super cool office in Bangalore where we brainstorm, ideate, debate, and execute. Aggressive and fast paced, we motivate ourselves with meaningful disputes over infinite cups of tea.

Product Details

  • Online Literature (Core Product) – 12 languages, 360K+ writers, 5M+ stories, 28M+ MAU, 3b minutes total time reading. We record 3M+ new followers, 10M+ new ratings, and 2.5M+ new reviews on the platform every month. We are larger than other Indian online literature platforms combined.
  • Pratilipi FM – Our stories are now in audio format across 9 languages, 5000+ Hours of Content, 300K MAU
  • Pratilipi Comics – Classic and in-house produced comics in 6 languages, 500+ Comic Series, 800K MAU
  • IVM Podcasts - Largest Podcasting Studio in India, with 5M+ listens a month
  • The Write Order - a progressive publication house which has published a collective of 23000+ authors in its 50+ anthologies and over 110+ solo books
  • Total Funding : $79M

*Role : *

We are looking for an experienced Talent Acquisition Specialist to help our company find and retain excellent team members. You will be entrusted with an ongoing quest to discover talent, build a strong employment brand, and ensure great relationships with both candidates and your stakeholders.

Sourcing :

  • As sourcing support, own sourcing strategies and outreach for critical roles.
  • Working closely with our internal stakeholders and understanding their hiring needs Helping them
  • in drafting JDs and driving the hiring end - to - end.
  • Handling outside sourcing partners and guiding them for the right fit.
  • Should be comfortable and effective at delivering optimal results in a fast-paced environment.
  • Engage and cultivate passive talent as we find the right opportunities for them.
  • Strong networking, mapping and referencing capabilities.
  • Should be able to maintain long term relationships with key talent in the industry.

Recruiting :

  • Responsible for recruiting /end to end searches for allocated roles
  • Execute full life cycle searches to include: developing the search strategy, responding to applicants, sourcing, prescreening and interviewing candidates to attract and evaluate candidates; develop and present recommendations on qualified candidates to our hiring managers.
  • Develop a proactive pipeline of a strong and diverse network of potential candidates.

Must Have :

  • 1-5 years of recruiting experience into Tech domain
  • Proven ability to develop and execute a comprehensive sourcing strategy and make use of consistent processes or methodologies that ensure consistent results
  • Problem Solving Skills
  • Demonstrated use of talent market insights and data to influence sourcing strategies
  • Experience with social media networking and employment branding initiatives
  • Technology Awareness.
  • Multitasking & ability to prioritize on a real time basis.
  • Must have worked on multiple Tech roles , handled senior level mandates.
