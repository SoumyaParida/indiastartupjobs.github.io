About Vymo: Who we are, What we do

Vymo is among the fastest growing enterprise SaaS startups in the world. In plain speak, our deal sizes are in 6 or 7 figures and what we do isn’t just convenient, it is critical to our customers.

For instance, if you turn off Vymo for a day - you halt business as usual for Asia’s largest Insurer, the 12th largest bank in the world, and 60 other large enterprises that depend on Vymo to enable salespeople and delight customers.

Customers don’t just buy our products off the shelf. It isn’t a see-click-buy-use experience, but a consult-solution-contract-deploy experience with significant investment from both sides. Our destinies are tied inextricably.

And with great power comes great responsibility. So, we don’t just hire people who can hack, break, or ship, we hire folks who can tinker, iterate, and solve meaningfully for our customers. And also, for us.

  • Title : Inside Sales Representative
  • Location: Bengaluru
  • Work Timing: Same as Target Market

Requirement

  • Motivated, self-starter with a desire to excel; Go-getter with ‘will figure out’ attitude
  • Experience: ISR (4 to 6 Years) Senior ISR (5 to 8 Years)

Job Function:

  • Inside sales would be responsible to open doors for sales reps to enter and sell. In this endeavor, reps are expected to conduct market research, gather account level intelligence, source their own leads, set up appointments, find opportunities, build pipeline, hard qualify and pass onto sales.
  • Additionally, account nurture for strategic accounts, independently running conversations and demo showcasing abilities would be critical to the role.

Responsibilities

  • Market Research: account, territory and industry vertical based - Insights driven research at account/contact level
  • Prospecting: Probe, Reach-out, Run discovery calls, stakeholder mapping
  • Scoping: Understand requirements, position Vymo and generate interest
  • Named account Navigation: Ability to navigate within identified accounts, use references to go deep and wide.
  • Account Presence: Establish trust with key contacts, establish high level of activity
  • Sourcing and scheduling meetings: Outbound centric appointment setting
  • Opportunity sourcing: identifying relevant opportunities based on Vymo fitment
  • Opportunity qualification: Criteria based - TEMP and BANT
  • Pipeline building: Ensuring a good funnel for steady opportunity flow
  • Sales Support: Targeted outreach to accelerate opportunity for sales reps - set up CXO and senior stakeholder meetings when required
  • Set the tone:· Present and articulate product features, Vymo value and benefits

Responsibilities

  • Communication: written and oral - Active listening, tonality, bonding & rapport building
  • Hustle: Hunger to learn, Motivation to win, experimentally curious
  • Confidence: Never say die spirit, Hunter mindset
  • Coachability: ability to learn new things, unlearn non-relevant practices
  • Demo Skills: Steady, smooth and engaging – generate interest and address concerns
  • Iteration: Ability to continuously repeat, try new approaches until goal is reached
  • Objection handling: Data driven, factual and confidence building
  • Time Management: Ability to multitask, work in a fast-paced environment

Requisites

  • Prior experience working in B2B, SAAS based start-ups
  • Outbound ability to independently source, qualify leads before transition to sales
  • Having prospected/sold into large enterprises specifically within IT
  • Experience in dealing with High ticket, low volume, long sales cycle sales environment
  • Inherent Inside sales skills – not too new with zero experience
  • Ability to connect the dots, client personas and research into a compelling pitch
  • Prior objection handling experience & critical thinking
  • Outstanding relationship building skills with high degree of responsiveness and integrity
  • Past Success: Demonstrated track record of consistently beating numbers on QoQ basis

Other Skills:

  • Team Player - · Energetic, upbeat, tenacious
  • Flexible to work in shifts (as per territory/region assigned)
  • Good technical know-how of SAAS industry
  • Geographical specialization – Relevant to specific Vymo aligned territories
  • Experience having sold SAAS based Sales productivity tools, CRM systems is a plus
