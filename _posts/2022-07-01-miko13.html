About Miko 3

Miko is an advanced consumer robotics company passionate about bringing the best of technology to young learners everywhere. We just launched Miko 3, our latest robot that uses playful, conversational learning to educate, engage and entertain kids. Our robots have been designed for ages 5+, and we actively sell in the US, UK, UAE, India, along with 140 other countries.

We are looking for a creative and result oriented individual to help drive growth and engagement of Miko Max subscription. The content specialist will be responsible for influencing the customer experience by using engaging content and communications that best support and communicate our best practice, insight-driven research. The person will work with business owners and cross functional teams to plan, coordinate and execute innovative marketing and communication initiatives to drive adoption among Miko’s users.

The ideal candidate is someone who executes creative problem solving and can translate marketing briefs into engaging communications. He/She is a Strong communicator (verbal and written), has exceptional attention to detail, capable of delivering error free content and can work as an individual contributor and a team player.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conducting in-depth research on industry-related topics in order to develop original content.
  • Developing content for all marketing communication and campaigns like Emails, Push notifications, blogs, articles, product descriptions, social media, and the company website.
  • Lead day-to-day operations of the Content Marketing team, including staging and publishing articles, white papers, ebooks and more.
  • Translate Product updates in user friendly blogs and emailers.
  • Ensure all-around brand consistency in the style, fonts, and images used, as well as the overall tone of the content.
  • Assist with monitoring and reporting on the performance of various content types to identify trends, benchmarks, bright spots and areas for improvement.
  • Drive and induce high engagement through creative communications.
  • Proofreading content for errors and inconsistencies.
  • Editing and polishing existing content to improve readability.
  • Conducting keyword research and using SEO best practices to increase traffic to the company website.
  • Identifying customers’ needs and recommending new content to address gaps in the company's current content.

Position Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master’s degree in communications, marketing, English, journalism, or related field.
  • You have 3 to 6 Years of experience in a content function designed to help drive a sales and marketing funnel preferably in e-commerce or kid’s space.
  • Proven content writing or copywriting experience for global audiences.
  • Working knowledge of content management systems.
  • You are good at measuring and analyzing.
  • You read voraciously.
  • The ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • The ability to handle multiple projects concurrently.
  • Effective communication skills.
