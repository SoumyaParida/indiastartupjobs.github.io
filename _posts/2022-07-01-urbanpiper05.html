Right from automating all their workflows across online delivery platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Deliveroo, UberEats—to building and deploying self-branded websites and apps, right to managing their walk-in customers and dine-in experiences, UrbanPiper is the preferred choice for over 20k+ restaurants.

The team:

The Sales Operations team is responsible for the overall productivity and effectiveness of the sales & marketing organization, partnering closely with Urban Piper’s Go-to-market teams to improve performance, drive revenue predictability and run programs across the organization to grow revenue. Sales & Marketing Strategy & Operations, Go to market Systems & Tools, Sales Enablement, Deal Desk and Sales Compensation are the functions under the purview of the Sales Operations team.

We are looking for an experienced leader with extensive Sales Operations and Go-to-market systems & tools experience to lead and build out each function within the Sales Operations team. This an exciting opportunity to build a critical function at Urban Piper from the ground-up!
