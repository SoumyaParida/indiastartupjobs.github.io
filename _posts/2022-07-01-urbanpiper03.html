Description

About the role

Performance Marketing plays a vital role in a B2B SaaS Marketing Strategy. We are looking for a qualified Performance Marketing Manager to join our team. You will be responsible for creating and executing a paid marketing strategy to meet the overall business goals. Our ideal candidate is an experienced professional with proven success in the field. As a Performance Marketing Manager, you should be able to strategise, consider various approaches, be data-driven and deliver results at scale. Ultimately, you should be able to oversee all performance marketing initiatives to ensure a strong pipeline of leads and build brand awareness in multiple geographies

What you’ll do

  • Create performance marketing strategies aligned with short-term and long-term marketing and business targets
  • Run campaigns on multiple platforms including Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, and more
  • Run campaigns in multiple geographies including India, UAE, and the UK
  • Managing budgets and campaigns across all digital channels to drive strong return on investment and efficient CAC
  • Identify channels for affiliate marketing and deploy a strategy to drive results with the same
  • Coordinating with a variety of agencies to supervise digital marketing campaigns, provide creative feedback, and augment internal marketing activities
  • Developing and managing digital prospecting and remarketing campaigns
  • Ensuring successful planning, execution, optimization for key traffic KPIs via paid, organic & own media channels
  • Identifying and testing new channels and tools to continue to meet or exceed established critical metrics
  • Create effective landing pages that move users down the funnel, drive conversion
  • Working closely with the relevant stakeholders to share funnel conversion improvement ideas, feedback & present results.
  • Coordinate with the rest of the marketing team to create and publish engaging content. (Website Content, Blogs, Case-studies, E-Books, Scripting for Podcasts and Branded Video Content, Sales Marketing Material, Marketing Collaterals, Emailers, Product Content etc)
  • Developing and implementing effective search engine optimization strategies
  • Stay up-to-date with developments and generate new ideas to draw the audience’s attention

  • Excellent analytical skills and the ability to leverage data, metrics, analytics and consumer behaviour trends to drive actionable insights & recommendations.

  • Basic technical knowledge of web publishing, creating effective landing pages
