Role Summary

As a Data Scientist, You will undertake data collection, preprocessing and analysis to build models to address business problems and represent information using data visualization techniques, understanding human behavior to determine what to look for in data. You will be at the forefront as this role would require strong collaboration across various teams and be the focal point for all stakeholders nearly in all functions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for analyzing and mining data to improve operational efficiency and drive optimization .
  • Be highly data driven and customer/operations focussed to understand opportunities for improvement through deep analysis.
  • Solving problems using statistical programming languages like Python/R to draw insights from large data sets.
  • Ability to use predictive models to increase utilization/revenue and optimize customer experience.
  • Build models for logistics optimization
  • Build models for supply chain optimization
  • Coordinate with different business verticals to implement new models and monitor outcomes.
  • Assess the effectiveness of the data and data sources.

Key Requirements:

  • 5+ years of experience in Data Science.
  • Strong problem solving skills
  • Knowledge of advanced statistical techniques and concepts.
  • Knowledge and experience working with statistical programming languages like Python/R
  • Experience creating and using advanced machine learning algorithms and statistics: regression, simulation, scenario analysis, modeling, clustering, decision trees, neural networks
  • Experience and knowledge in data mining techniques
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
  • A journey that will challenge and reward you in multiple ways.
