Role Summary

As a Devops Engineer, you will be an integral part of a DevOps team managing the daily operations of this fast-growing start-up.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Enable provisioning to a fast-paced dev & test team through IaC (Infra as code)
  • Develop Infrastructure-as-code artifacts for re-use across the environments.
  • Create and manage secure images
  • Proactively monitor and check for active attacks and work on prevention methods. Develop and implement steps towards ensuring a higher level of security of the systems.
  • Monitor and check abnormal functioning of the systems
  • Actively participate in managing Build & Release activities.
  • Assist in designing and managing systems for HA/DR
  • Ensure availability, performance, security, and scalability of production systems.
  • Drive automation and compliance of configuration using the relevant tooling.
  • Security Hardening of cloud infrastructure application/web server against known/unknown vulnerabilities.

Key Requirements:

  • Docker, AWS, Linux, Kubernete and ECS
  • Prior experience provisioning and deployment in Kubernetes
  • Experience API gateway and Service mesh
  • Experience supporting a managed cloud services infrastructure
  • Ability to maintain, monitor and optimise production database servers
  • In-depth knowledge on Linux Environment.
  • Designing and implementing highly efficient solutions on Pubic Cloud (Amazon Web Services) for security, resilience, performance, networking, availability, Blue-green deployments in context of business application.
  • Good working knowledge of AWS services such as Messaging, Application Services, Migration Services, Cost Management Platform.
  • Experience in capacity planning & design, cost and effort estimations and experience in Infra cost optimization.

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
