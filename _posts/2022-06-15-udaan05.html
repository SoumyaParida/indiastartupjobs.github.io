udaan is a B2B ecommerce platform that brings traders, wholesalers, distributors, retailers, and manufacturers in India on to a single platform. Our goal is to make ‘doing business’ easy in India & make B2B commerce convenient and efficient.

udaan Capital is the NBFC arm of udaan & offers credit services to buyers and sellers on and off udaan’s B2B ecommerce platform to grow their business further. This space is largely untapped with less than 10% of MSMEs having access to working capital loans from financial institutions. In this way, udaan is uniquely positioned to formalize &scale trade credit.

Learn more about us: https://udaan.com/

What are we looking for?

The Strategic Partnerships team is the growth engine of udaan Capital and is responsible for building a large scale, sustainable and high-quality SME loan book by partnering with Enterprises as an Anchor. The role will require extensive networking within our target industries by engaging with CXOs and Founders to build a healthy deal pipeline and ensure timely closure of Anchor Partnerships

Job Responsibilities

  • Acquiring Mid to Large scale Anchors (Brands, Platforms, Manufacturers etc.) for providing invoice financing solutions for their Distributors, Dealers and Retailers.
  • Being the brand ambassador of udaan and udaanCapital - demonstrate the highest level of integrity, responsiveness and customer centricity and tirelessly advocate for adoption of products/services of udaanCapital and udaan with anchor partners.
  • Networking with CXOs and other decision makers across industry engagement forums to generate industry insights and build innovative industry specific GTM strategies
  • Responsible for portfolio building and managing the network of leads received via anchors by coordinating with the Sales and Central Underwriting teams
  • Maintaining the health of the portfolio and ensuring profitability of the loan book
  • Partnering with key stakeholders of the Anchors and growing the relationship to capture higher penetration of the program
  • Collaborating with Product, Technology and Policy teams to improve the value proposition of the product and further improve the user experiences

KRAs & KPIs of the role

  • Anchor Acquisition - Acquiring Mid to Large scale brands/platforms for invoice financing solutions
  • Book Building – Partnering with Sales team effectively to drive higher conversion of leads to borrowers and driving utilisation and AUM building by coordinating with Anchor
  • P&L Management – Negotiating right commercials with Anchors, optimising the lead to borrower conversions, and keeping delinquencies under tight control by effective program management by partnering with internal and external stakeholders

Functional Competencies

  • Customer Centricity and Problem Solving: Understanding the industry nuances and customer requirements in detail and thereby offering customized lending solutions
  • Stakeholder Management: Ability to collaborate with diverse external and internal stakeholders to onboard and scale Anchor programs
  • Cross Functional Integration: Coordinate with Sales, Central Underwriting and Product teams to continuously upgrade the Customer Value Propositions and drive better user experience
  • Perseverance: Individual need to be confident, self-starter and enthusiastic with a strong sense of self-discipline, drive, and urgency

Candidate Profile

  • 5-8 years of B2B Sales/Partnerships background with strong understanding of the corporate landscape in India
  • Proven track record of acquiring brands/platforms and familiarity with navigating complex organisations to identify decision makers and closing deals
  • Demonstrate excellent stakeholder management and collaboration skills to work in a highly fast paced environment
  • Passionate about problem solving with 1st principle approach keeping customer as the focus
  • Basic understanding of Supply Chain Financing and SME lending would be a plus
