ABOUT THE TEAM & ROLE:

Founded in 2014, Swiggy is India’s leading tech-driven on-demand delivery platform with a vision to elevate the quality of life for the urban consumer by offering unparalleled convenience. The platform is engineered to connect millions of consumers with hundreds of thousands of restaurants and stores across 500+ cities. Our phenomenal growth has come on the back of great technology, incredible innovation, and sound decision-making.

Our Analytics team comprises product & data analysts who focus on improving customer/product/driver/restaurant experience by providing top-line reporting solutions, ML, business intelligence analytics, and strategic solutions to business and product teams by providing actionable insights, Identifying potential opportunities. The Analytics function operates in a centralized manner and cuts through all business, engineering, product, and design teams.

This partners with decision-makers to solve problems across our entire value chain. Improving menus for our restaurant partners, developing incentives for delivery executives, recommending the best channel for marketing to consumers, and predicting demand in hyperlocal geographies are all examples of the repertoire of projects that we undertake. If decoding hyperlocal excites you then come, and join us on this journey.

What qualities are we looking for?

We’re currently looking for the leader of Analytics for Swiggy with 14-20 years of leadership experience in managing analytics functions across a spectrum of product, strategy & operational ecosystems.

She/He will be responsible for converting data and insights into business value.

This leader should bring excellent strategic thinking and analytical frameworks to create the vision for Swiggy analytics, uplevel the team and current operating model, and expertise in building high-performing teams. Thought leadership, sound judgment, & technical prowess will form the core of this role. This leader would be expected to accelerate the culture by taking decisions based on data and creating an impact through data. She/ He will have :

  • Bachelor's/Master’s (preferred) degree in Business Administration, Engineering, Economics, Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related quantitative field
  • 14-20 years of overall experience working in analytics/ decision sciences with at least 8 years managing large teams, and being a leader of leaders
  • Comfort in re-defining frontiers and passion for doing cutting-edge work enabling data-oriented decision-making for the organization
  • Driving conceptual technical discussions with Leaders from engineering, Product Management, Business, CXOs
  • Analytical and strategic thinker with experience creating systems and solving problems at scale using data
  • Comfortable working with ambiguity, unknown variables, and missing context (and find ways to bridge those gaps, when necessary)
  • Outstanding oral and written communication skills, the ability to articulate and evangelize product vision, and ‘Go to market plans’ in a compelling storytelling manner
  • Expert at negotiating complex matters amidst conflicting sources of information and diverse opinions. Rally key stakeholders to commit resources to the product vision and take decisions in a collaborative way
  • Demonstrated Leadership virtues and the founder’s mentality, long-term, and solution-oriented mindset

What will you get to do here?

  • Lead and nurture a high-performing team that thrives on excellence
  • Grow the Analytics team both through hiring and coaching, training and mentoring existing team members
  • Define the culture and ways of working for the analytics team, be a leader of leaders
  • Maintain a customer-centric focus: strive to be a domain and product authority through data, develop trust among the leaders
  • Be an advocate for data-driven everything with the ability to focus on the big picture takeaways of any analysis while being able to speak to the underlying details
  • Identify the right metrics/measurement frameworks to track progress against given org priorities
  • Set high standards in execution; own scope and timelines for the tenure spectrum of the Swiggy Analytics team
  • Take responsibility for skill-building within the organization (training, process definition, research of new tools and techniques, etc.)
  • Plan team sizing and own hiring to meet the required size
  • Set performance benchmarks for the team; provide feedback and appraise the team at regular intervals
