We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favorite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

JioSaavn is seeking a highly motivated Associate Manager to join our dynamic Programming team. You will work closely with the Programming Head and provide support on content programming and curation. You will be responsible for identifying and curating excellent music listening and programming experiences for a wide array of moods and genres. In this role, you will also get the opportunity to demonstrate a keen understanding of performance-oriented analytics and have your finger on the music pulse.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Assist in managing the daily Telugu programming and curation strategy. Working knowledge of Kannada + Malayalam is a big plus.
  • Work with Global Programming Teams as well as other cross-functional teams to achieve various organizational objectives
  • Act as a liaison between various departments to ensure the on-time and orderly flow of work
  • Curate and maintain a substantial number of varied playlists based on different Moods, Genres, and Activities.
  • Think of creative ideas and concepts for Festivals, events and Trending Topics
  • Stay on top of new music releases
  • Help find ways to organize, facilitate, and improve general programming processes
  • Create and update departmental calendar and project status.
  • Keep the entire team informed on the current status of all jobs in progress via status meetings
  • Keep abreast of all work in the Programming Department and follow up on due dates that were established for copy, artwork, playlists, and other deliverables

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s / Master’s degree
  • 3-5 years experience in the music industry, ideally programming for music services, working with a broad range of music content
  • Passionate about music and have deep knowledge of music culture (Telugu).
  • Highly organized, creative self-starter with experience working on multiple projects at the same time
  • Care about attention to detail and organization, have a team spirit, strong work ethic, and a sense of humor
  • Exceptional time management and prioritization skills
  • Out-of-the-box thinking and ideas that can significantly contribute to JioSaavn’s success.
