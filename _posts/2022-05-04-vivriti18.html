About the team and role

Part of Investment - Credit team at Vivriti Asset Management. Role would include portfolio investment, monitoring and research for various debt schemes to be managed by Vivriti AMC.

Primary Responsibilities

Fund deployment phase:

  • Conduct due diligences, prepare investment notes, seek IC approvals
  • Seek, analyze, and structure data provided by clients
  • Thorough field diligence & meetings with the senior management of investee entities
  • Prepare insightful credit report for presentation to Investment Committee with cogent recommendations

Post deployment phase:

  • Identifying early warning signs of credit deterioration
  • Investment monitoring, field visit, management meetings, calls, etc.
  • Regular Updates to Investment Committee
  • Suggestions on portfolio Rebalancing

Credit Research:

  • Track assigned sectors closely and publish regular research reports to internal and external stakeholders

Desired Skills and Experience

  • CA/CFA/MBA
  • Possess 1-3 years of relevant credit assessment experience i.e.,
    • structured credit/lending to mid corporates and FIs or
    • worked in equity research / credit underwriting / credit rating or
    • worked in portfolio / credit monitoring teams of NBFCs, Banks, PEs, debt funds etc
    • worked in due diligence teams of Big 4s or worked at a rating agency
  • Be brilliant with numbers, analysing finanical statmetns, managing and interpreting data and solving problems - to aid in making investment decisions
  • Have excellent report writing skills
  • Be as ambitious as we are, and hence be willing to get out of her / his way to burn the midnight oil to meet targets and deadlines
