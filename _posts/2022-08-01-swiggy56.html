Regulatory Challenge responsibilities:

  • Ensure monitoring and management of regulatory compliance, labeling, product registration, and food safety in Instamart supply chain PAN INDIA level.

  • Should conduct assessments on regulatory changes and coordinate with the business to develop and implement impactful supply chain.

  • Should be able to provide advice to develop regulatory framework to category and catalogue, marketing teams on regulatory compliance.
  • Ensure that Senior Management is informed and updated regarding emerging regulatory issues and risks in matters of regulatory compliance.
