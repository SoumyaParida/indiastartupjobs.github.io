Learning Consultant : 6 Months Contract (with Tech Talent Development team)

Job Responsibilities

  • Drive the design, creation and execution of learning efforts to support Swiggy Tech teams needs
  • Program manage Monthly learning calendar creation and execution by working with Tech HR team and Tech stakeholders
  • Generate and execute ideas to improve adoption of our asynchronous learning content available on various learning content platforms that we use
  • Ensure that all learning initiatives are marketed, communicated to relevant cohorts and Month end learning report is generated and shared with stakeholders
  • Cross-functional collaboration to create new content/leverage existing learning solutions
  • Adherence to set standards to deliver, track and evaluate learning solutions
  • Ensure that courses are set up and uploaded to the LMS (degreed) accurately and within the stipulated timelines
  • Create documentation and project plans of Learning initiatives
  • Create and publish a monthly dashboard on L&D initiatives . eg number of unique users, participants, feedback scores, total hours in learning
  • Utilize recent industry experience to make appropriate design decisions
  • Ability to drive high quality learner focused programs

Education & Experience:

  • Minimum 4-5 years of relevant experience (into Learning and Development)
  • Experience with Tech teams will be an added advantage

Required Skills

  • Proficient in designing and developing training content, with stakeholder partnerships (both instructor led as well as self paced modules)
  • Good understanding of Instructional Design principles and best practices
  • Excellent communication & presentation skills
  • Proficient in time management and working independently

Preferred Skills & Knowledge

Ability to apply adult learning principles in training design and delivery
