Role Summary

You will be working as a part of the data-driven Data Visualization team which is primarily responsible for leveraging visualization tools to create informative dashboards and help the company to make impactful decisions. The role is focused on leveraging Tableau to create visually appealing reports, charts, graphs and dashboards using data. The role may require driving, facilitating ongoing daily/weekly monthly reviews in a highly unstable environment calling out insights on improvement areas for delivery leads.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Own the design, development, and maintenance of ongoing reports, metrics, analyses, dashboards to drive key business decisions
  • Make recommendations for new metrics, techniques, and strategies to improve further in future
  • Enable effective decision making by retrieving and compiling data in digestible format from multiple sources
  • Put on your business thinking cap to drive insights and action we can take to improve returns compliance and experience

Key Requirements:

  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets and provide reports & visualization
  • Work experience in SQL, Python, Knowledge of Hive and Data warehousing concepts will be a plus point
  • Work with management to prioritize business KPIs and information needs
  • Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
  • Technical expertise with data models, database design and development, data mining and segmentation techniques
  • Proven success in a collaborative, team-oriented environment
  • Working experience with geospatial data will be a plus

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
  • A journey that will challenge and reward you in multiple ways.
