About Yulu

Are you sick of breathing poor quality air in our cities? Do you worry about climate change? Have you felt like pulling your hair on seeing a congested red route on the Google map?

Did you nod in ‘yes’? Same here, we did too! And so, we at Yulu set out to fix all of these issues by making our mobility smart, efficient and sustainable.

Being India’s leading shared electric mobility player with the largest fleet of EVs and biggest Battery-as-a-Service network, Yulu is reimagining urban mobility for a better future. Not only do we provide an affordable, sustainable and accessible commute alternative to daily commuters, but we are also making last-mile goods deliveries cheaper and green. Simply put, we are revolutionizing the way urban India commutes and consumes by building the Mobility 2.0 paradigm.

We are innovating at the intersection of consumer behaviour, public policies, urban infrastructure, cutting edge technology, deep learning, product design, supply chain innovation and economic inclusion. Our proprietary mobility platform uses IoT, Machine Learning, and AI for vehicle access, fleet management, intelligent demand-supply management, and efficient operations. After 3 years of building a strong foundation for electric mobility, now, as we are ready to feverishly scale our business 10x this year and 100x over the next 3 years by working on many new, complex & BIG challenges on technology, data, product, markets, customer segments and growth, we still feel we are just getting started!

If our mission brings a spark in your eyes & if you’d like to join some of the most passionate people in the country who are committed to transform the cacophony on Indian roads into a symphony - Come, join the #Unstoppable Yulu team!!

Job Summary

As a Product Head, you will be responsible for the success of multiple tech platforms at YULU. You will ideate, strategize, define and develop product roadmap as well as ensure product delivery. You will manage a team of product Managers and work closely with the marketing, operations and the revenue team.

Key Responsibilities:

PRODUCT STRATEGY AND ROADMAP

  • Develop product strategy and define steps required to bring the product to fruition.
  • Be the primary driver of YULU products, oversee all aspects of product strategy, development and management. Work closely with the founders, the engineering team, marketing and other stakeholders to arrive at a clear product roadmap in line with the vision of the organization.
  • Manage every aspect of product’s development from conception to completion.

INNOVATION

  • Take ownership of product innovation and improvement. Ensure that the product is constantly growing to meet the needs of customers and other stakeholders.
  • Generate new product ideas, enhancements to existing products by collating requests from both internal stakeholders and customers.

PRODUCT EVALUATION/ ANALYSIS

  • Highlight risks, bring up impacts and assist in analysing possible solutions for complex issues.
  • Identify issues in the product roadmap, assist the team in resolving possible issues to ensure the goals of the product are Clearly met.

COLLABORATION

  • Manage a team of product Managers and work closely with the founders & engineering team
  • Conduct Product demos. Be a Product advocate; describe the Product, its value, its working and adoption. Explain upcoming changes and tailor the information to the audience
  • Work in a matrix environment to deliver a product that aligns with the needs of all stakeholders.

Key Requirements:

  • BTech / MTech and equivalent practical knowledge
  • 2- 5 years of experience in Product Management experience in the logistics or mobility industry. Exposure to products related to people and goods mobility would be an advantage.
  • Experience building and managing teams
  • Excellent problem-solving capabilities, effective communication and interpersonal skills
  • Comfortable operating in a fast-paced, rapidly-evolving workplace
  • In-depth understanding of the Agile principles and methodologies and how they’re applied in workplace culture

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
  • A journey that will challenge and reward you in multiple ways.
