What you will do at Vymo

As a very valuable member of the Global Recruitment Team, you will enable rapid growth in Vymo across locations. You will champion Recruitment Best Practices and Process knowledge and act as a Talent Partner to the hiring teams. We are looking for a recruitment professional who is passionate about solving complex recruitment problems and setting up things from scratch.

A Day in your Life

  • You will manage the full cycle recruitment process for technical and non-technical roles across the organization
  • You will partner with the Hiring Manager to identify multi-channel hiring strategies to meet the hiring goals
  • You will collaborate to define roles, act as the sourcing expert for different job families, screen candidates for culture and quality fit and finally close them
  • You will own our pipeline strategy and hire passive candidates by building and maintaining a network of potential candidates, through proactive market research and on-going relationship management
  • You will ensure great candidate experience and raise the bar with each candidate hired
  • You will recommend ideas and strategies related to recruitment that will contribute to the long-range growth of the organisation
  • You will participate in special projects and own recruiting initiatives, including Hiring Best Practices, Diversity and Inclusion initiatives, Talent Analytics
  • You will bring in insights by analysing data related to all facets of the hiring process, including sourcing effectiveness, conversation rates, and other Key KPIs
  • You will build strong partnerships with all key hiring stakeholders, globally and HR generalists
