About OYE! Rickshaw

Oye! Rickshaw is building a shared, electric, tech-based, micro-mobility ecosystem for the real Bharat. We solve people's daily commute problems, making it a seamless, reliable, affordable and convenient experience for our customers. We do it all through our 100% plug-in electric rickshaws. We provide opportunities for drivers to do deliveries on their e-rickshaws for our B2B and B2C clients to further increase their income, as well as conveniently access cheap energy via our energy infrastructure.

Why Oye! Rickshaw?

We are working on some of the most complex problems in micro-mobility, electric vehicles. You will be challenging yourself everyday and learn along the way.

Our team is still small and you will get a chance to work on most interesting and impactful problems. You'd be wearing many hats participating in every phase of development. Absolutely no boredom.

You will be working in a very fast growing early stage startup. You will get a chance to grow as an Oyepreneur very quickly.

We are backed by marquee investors such as Matrix Partners and Chiratae Ventures
