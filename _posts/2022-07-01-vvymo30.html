Job brief

We are looking to hire Growth Marketing Executives to run with our marketing strategies. The expectation from this role will include running experiments across the funnel, improving conversion rates, and working cross-department to collaborate on new initiatives.

If you are interested in using systematic ways to test experiments and can generate innovative ideas to increase sales opportunity pipeline, then we would love to have a chat with you. As a growth marketer your core responsibility will be to innovate, execute and analyze the channels and strategies to increase engagement, create opportunities and meet the company goals.

As a Growth Marketer at Vymo, you will:

  • Develop and execute the growth plan in conjunction with the senior executive team
  • Create and deliver marketing collaterals to run campaigns
  • Identify opportunities to implement and then innovate world-class campaigns
  • Identify and develop channel opportunities with technology partners
  • Own and execute events, webinars and performance marketing campaigns
  • Own and execute affiliate marketing and community building
  • Report, recommend and iterate on campaigns to drive increased ROI
  • Identify opportunities, threats and challenges in markets and act accordingly
  • Working closely with data, sales and product

What are we looking for in an ideal Growth Marketer:

  • A belief and determination to solve complex problems
  • A strong understanding and passion for all things digital marketing
  • A highly creative, data-driven and analytical mind
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Excellent written communication skills
  • A willingness to fail-forward, learn and grow
