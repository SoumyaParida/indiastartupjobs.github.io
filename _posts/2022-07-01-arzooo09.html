The recent foray into fintech has enabled us to empower Indian retail with a low cost, digital-first Credit alternative, which is unlocking immense scale for Arzooo.

Through the direct to consumer platform we're taking all these stores online with Live Commerce capabilities, building appliances focused shopping experience for tier-2+ India. We're a team of 600+ Arzooites, with deep experience in scaling startups, and we are building this team further now.
