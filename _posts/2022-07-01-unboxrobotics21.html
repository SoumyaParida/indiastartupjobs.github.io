Purpose:

Systems Engineering is a cross-functional engineering team that is responsible for planning and execution of design and development processes. This team will work along with hardware and software teams to generate the system level requirements. Systems engineering team is also responsible to plan and execute integration and testing activities to ensure that the systems meet the performance metrics.

In this team you will be working with various systems and subsystems like sensing and actuation systems that would be critical in the robots and robotic systems for automating the warehouse operations.
