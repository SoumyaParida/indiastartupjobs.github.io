Do You Make The Cut?

Are you passionate about delivering great products? Are you familiar with the latest cloud computing technologies?

Then you might be the right team member we are looking for!

You will work closely with our development teams from the early stages of design, all the way through identifying and resolving production issues. You will help to develop new features and writing scripts for automation and streamlining the company's operations, processes, and in charge of the specification and documentation of the new project features.

Responsibilities:

  • As a Lead DevOps Engineer, you will mentor engineers and build automation tools for provisioning and managing our cloud infrastructure by leveraging the AWS/Azure.
  • Design and build the CI/CD pipelines & integration with the team.
  • You must proactively seek out opportunities and contribute to enhance and manage various infrastructure services such as monitoring, orchestration, and continuous delivery pipelines.
  • Involve in peer review of infra scripting & architecture.
  • Work closely with software development teams to build reliable, and scalable services that power our products.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to networking, DNS using Route 53, and other system tools.
  • Work in parallel with infosec team regarding audit and compliance process (preferably PCI-DSS)
  • Involved in cross-team interaction for designing the high-level architecture for new products/applications.
  • You will also handle critical support issues along with the SRE team.

Sounds Like You?

  • 4+ years of experience with Unix/Linux and comfortable in scripting in Python, Shell or Bash for deployment and management.
  • 4+ years of experience with AWS Core Services including VPC, EC2, S3, Route 53, RDS.
  • Hands-on experience with Azure including VNet, Subscriptions, load balancers, storage.
  • 1+ years of experience in mentoring the engineers.
  • Expert in Python, Ansible & Terraform.
  • Strong experience in deploying, automation/configuration management using either Puppet, Chef, or Jenkins.
  • Strong experience in Continuous Integration (CI)/ Continuous Delivery (CD).
  • Managing and configuring monitoring systems using Nagios, Zabbix, Grafana, time-series databases (influx DB, graphite), etc.
  • Experience with SIEM/ELK and Serverless Architecture
  • Experience with IPS & IDS.
  • Strong experience in Docker & Kubernetes environment
  • In-depth knowledge of scalability and reliability engineering.
  • Firm grasp of the Agile SDLC methodology and practices.
