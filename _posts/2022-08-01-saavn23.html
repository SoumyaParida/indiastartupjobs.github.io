ROLE:

  • Functional lead in Revenue and Accounts receivable management
  • Actively working with business and ensuring seamless management of functions
  • Support the company in optimizing financial transactions and systems by performing reconciliation of payments and customer accounts
  • Communicate discrepancies to the management team, identify slow-paying customers, and recommend collection candidates
  • Strengthen and grow relationships with clients by communicating with customers regarding past-due accounts
  • Assist in streamlining and improving the accounts receivable process identifying areas of performance improvement
  • Exercise integrity and confidentiality in financial reporting, and comply with local financial requirements

RESPONSIBILITES:

Accounts Receivable & Collections

  • Preparing outstanding statement & ageing report
  • Maintain accounts receivable records to ensure aging is up to date, credits and collections are applied, uncollectible amounts are accounted for, and miscellaneous differences are cleared
  • Following-up with customer for payments
  • Preparing KPI data for Revenue and Accounts receivable as per management requirement

Invoicing, Reporting & MIS for company's revenue streams

  • Ensuring invoices preparation along with proper documentation in co-ordination with the Business team for multiple streams of revenue i.e. Ad, Subscription, Network, etc
  • Ensuring timely invoices is sent to the customer and resolving queries
  • Recording Sales Invoices in accounts
  • Recording collection entries in accounts
  • Revenue reporting to management as per the requirement
  • Continuous discussion with business operation team
  • Providing required revenue data to business team based on company requirement

Working with Brand Solutions & Business Development Teams

  • Preparing Volume discount working
  • Sharing Consumption & receipts data for incentives calculation
  • Helping AM with Vendor and Customer Registration Form
  • Sharing of Revenue and Billing data with internal team

Supporting Finance & Accounts teams

  • Leading Revenue and AR function
  • Leading a SOP and automation drive
  • Assisting Finance and Accounts team in group reporting and compliance
  • Active business partnering with business team and ensuring problem-solving
  • Audit Support (Internal Audit, Statutory Audit)
  • Working as one team

REQUIREMENTS:

  • B.com or other Finance Domain
  • 5 to 8 years of Revenue and Collections AR experience
  • Understanding basic principles of finance, accounting, and bookkeeping
  • Superb time management and detail
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook) and Google drive
  • Ability to keep company and partner confidences
  • Proficient at Tally, Quickbooks, Salesforce etc.
