Pepper (www.peppercontent.io) is building a global content marketplace that brings companies and content creators together to scale up content creation processes across 25 content verticals and 300+ content categories. Over the past 4 years, we’ve worked with 2000+ brands like Amazon India, Adobe, Swiggy, Dunzo, Businessworld, Times Group, Digit, YourStory, BookMyShow, UpGrad, Paisabazaar, Zivame, Yatra, Bewakoof, etc. to create content that is meticulous and customized according to our customers’ needs.

We are on the path to building a global on-demand content marketplace platform that allows for collaboration, discovery and upskilling for all our creators. Join us to become a part of the content revolution!

As a creative lead, you'll be part of our in-house marketing team in Mumbai and oversee all the creative efforts of the social media, design, and video teams.

Objectives of this Role

  • Build, lead, and review work of the social media, design, and video team in the production of all content collaterals.
  • Spearhead and manage content strategy and set and maintain creative standards for all the departments.
  • Creating a vision for Pepper’s internal content creation efforts and directing the respective teams to execute it.
  • Collaborate with Business and Supply teams to translate marketing objectives into onboarding more creators and businesses on our platform.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Oversee a cross-functional social media, design, and video team in the strategic development of messages and deliverables.
  • Focus on how social media can become a channel for not only engaging with the community but also bringing in more supply (creators on the platform) and demand (business on the platform)
  • Prioritize work and resources across engagements based on short- and long-term needs, establishing content creation and distribution schedules by collaborating with other teams.
  • Review and approve art and copy developed by the team, ensuring deliverables that effectively address marketing goals and challenges.
  • Conduct brainstorming sessions with the creative teams, maintaining strategic and creative thinking to develop innovative and actionable content in a structured manner

Skills and Qualifications

  • 5+ years of related experience including 5+ years of experience leading creative teams in an agency or corporate setting.
  • Highly skilled in leading a team of creative talent.
  • Strong creative vision with an understanding of business objectives.
  • Strong eye for detail in terms of copy, design, and video.
