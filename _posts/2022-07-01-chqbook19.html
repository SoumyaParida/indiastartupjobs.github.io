Job Overview:

The Team Leader's responsibility is to ensure individual agents are performing against targets, while reviewing their performance and coaching them to do so. The TL will be responsible for handling tele agents on the floor in Chqbook Contact Centre through direct interaction with them with the goal of motivating, coaching and mentoring them to improve sales and individual’s productivity and performance. These interactions are very important for the company as they are the first step in the ladder of the complete process of the sales. WE at Chqbook.com believe that Team leaders are highly motivated people with confidence, integrity, knowledge and problem solving.
