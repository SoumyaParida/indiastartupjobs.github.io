  • Prepare important MIS reports and PowerPoint Presentations for the management.
  • Respect the need for confidentiality and sensitivity of the information.
  • Filter general information, handling queries, phone calls, and invitations to the CEO by redirecting or forwarding such contact as appropriate.
  • Facilitating and managing logistics for key events and projects to include: onsite/offsite employee functions, team building, all-hands meetings, performance reviews, operational planning meetings, and space allocation.
  • Coordinate internal and external meetings greet visitors/ arrange logistics for the meeting/ setting up meeting rooms, VC & Conference calls etc. Timely reminder of the meeting schedule.

  • Minimum 3 years of related work experience as an Executive Assistant role
  • Advanced problem solving and planning skills to manage deliverables and timelines
  • Superb organizational skills to manage high-level communication with both internal and external stakeholders
  • Ability to communicate at all levels of the organization, driven, motivated to continually learn, the role has a deep career runway
  • Ability to successfully manage complex, fast-moving, and unexpected communications & challenges in order to deliver stellar results
