About Vymo

Vymo builds world class enterprise products for automating sales force activities and driving sales effectiveness. We partner with our clients to help them institute cutting edge sales practices into their daily routine through delightfully simple tools. Our vision is to help our clients achieve an unprecedented degree of sales productivity and effectiveness and we bring together the combined experience of our team to achieve this.

We have an opportunity to impact the way lakhs (if not millions) of sales force folks interact with customers and improve their outcomes. We also have the unique opportunity to play with millions of location data points we collect in this process and lead the innovation in the space of localytics and data analytics.

We are growing at an amazing pace and expanding our delivery team. Come be a part of this exciting journey and create new, innovative products.

Responsibilities

  • Set the long-term vision and strategy for the core Vymo platform by defining product requirements and specifications
  • Understand, articulate and spec features that have highest impact across customers
  • Build consensus with the engineering team on the highest impact items while maintaining a high quality bar
  • Balance customer satisfaction and user delight
  • Partner with Sales, Marketing, UX and Support teams< to shape the product roadmap and drives business growth
  • Deliver product functionality that meets the scalability and security requirements of Enterprise customers in developed markets (US and APAC)

Required Skills

  • Must have shipped core platform pieces used in other parts of the product / other product teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication demonstrated in conversations spanning developers & executive stakeholders
  • Interfaced with real-world user data, customer feedback, and metrics to define and shape product quality
  • Familiarity with Engineering tools & processes
