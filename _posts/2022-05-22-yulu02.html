Role Summary

As a UI Designer, you would collaborate with product management and engineering to define and implement innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals and experience. You would be executing all visual design stages from the concept to final hand-off to engineering apart from conceptualizing original ideas that bring simplicity and user friendliness to complex design roadblocks.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for all product releases. Ensure timely, stable and well-tested releases including the android app, web app and backend releases
  • Devise and manage QA processes
  • Coordinate with product managers, design team, engineering team, and the QA team to deliver results.
  • Pioneer the designing of Dashboards.
  • Facilitate sprint planning, retrospective and other scrum meetings. Help the team estimate and increase velocity.
  • Monitor the progress of the sprint, devise process and suggest improvements regularly

Key Requirements:

  • Minimum of 2+ years of experience as a visual designer, user experience designer, interaction designer, or similar role.
  • An innate capacity for creating engaging, highly usable designs and layouts
  • Understand product specifications and user psychology while designing
  • Ability to quickly turn around rigorous wireframes and mockups in a visual design.
  • Experience creating style guides, pattern libraries, and other design solutions that drive design across organizations.
  • Experience working with agile development teams.
  • An online portfolio or samples of work demonstrating experience creating great user-centered design solutions and UI design expertise involving Dashboard designing.
  • Experience in a variety of design and prototype tools such as Sketch, Figma, Photoshop and Wireframing.
  • An innate capacity for creating engaging, highly usable designs and layouts
  • Understand product specifications and user psychology while designing
