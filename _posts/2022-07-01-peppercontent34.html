Pepper (www.peppercontent.in) is building a global content marketplace that brings companies and content creators together to scale up content creation processes across various content verticals and more than 150 content categories. Over the past 3.5 years, we’ve worked with 2500+ brands like Adani, HDFC Bank, India Today, Google India, Amazon India, Adobe, Swiggy, Dunzo, Unacademy etc. to create content that is meticulous and customised according to our their needs.

We are on the path to building a global on-demand content marketplace platform and bring together the best creative talent across the world and utilise them for our customer’s creative content needs. Now we are in a rapid expansion phase to enter into more content categories and engage a wide spectrum of content creators, and to facilitate all our ambitions, we are looking for an experienced Delivery Lead who has a high PnL ownership, keen eye for operational vision and an analytical mind to identify problems and streamline the processes.

Roles and Responsibilities

Operations management

  • Daily project tracking, priority alignment for the ops team
  • Maximum realization of contracted work
  • Crisis management - bulk rework, pilots, and sample
  • Ensuring all the reworks happen through the platform
  • Designer payment and corresponding support queries

(Bulk Project) Set up and implementation

  • Customer onboarding
  • Template setting according to all customer feedback - for ease of ordering and driving platform usage
  • Identify and fix costing and level of designers who will work

Supply planning

  • Track upcoming projects with Business Team
  • Analyze the performance of current supply - coordinate supply redressal
  • Estimate supply requirements based on the current pool and upcoming projects
  • Coordinate with supply team and application reviewers for supply acquisition

Day-to-day dealing with revenue team

  • Best case pricing for bulk projects
  • Expectation setting and project onboarding
  • Deadline prediction for bulk projects
  • Execution of high priority, sample, and urgent projects
  • Quality escalation management

The ideal candidate must have:

  • Have 5+ years of working experience in project management
  • Have demonstrated success in defining and scaling cross-stakeholder projects
  • Be comfortable in an environment that is constantly changing, frequently uncertain, and at times chaotic
  • Strong problem-solving skills and willingness to roll up one’s sleeves to get the job
  • Be able to lead cross-discipline discussions with influence and not formal authority
  • Very sound communication skills to listen and articulate opinions of all the stakeholders
  • Collaborate with Persuasiveness
  • Have strong attention to detail and the ability to handle multiple activities simultaneously
  • Keen to learn, mentor and willing to take ownership of work items
