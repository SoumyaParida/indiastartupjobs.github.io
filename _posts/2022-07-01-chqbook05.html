Job Function:

Product Management

Job Title

Product Manager

Reports To

Sr Product Manager

What will your role look like?

  • Manage the entire lifecycle of the product from conception to final release and ensure that it can handle the complexities of the business.
  • Will work on consumer-facing products as well as internal system applications like CRM & LMS - which drive the telesales and customer service teams.
  • Work with Business stakeholders to understand the requirements and helping them drive revenue and customer experience using the systems you own.
  • Write PRDs, Create mock-ups, Define success metrics for all product features
  • Partner with design, engineering, and other functions to develop and deliver the best-in-class product with a pragmatic approach.
  • Monitor and measure success metrics of products and features
  • Conduct research to identify customer needs and market gaps

Who are we looking for?

  • Engineering background(B.Tech, BE)
  • 4+ years of experience with at least 2 years of Product Management experience
  • Experience in end-to-end product management in software or web technologies
  • Excellent analytical skills with great attention to detail
  • Excellent verbal, written, presentation and interpersonal communication and relationship skills
  • Experience working with Call Center Telephony solutions is preferred.

About Chqbook:

Chqbook.com is building India’s first SME focussed Neo Bank for small business owners (across SMEs, HoReCa and Kiranas). We are helping these small business owners through our five pillars of Lending, Credit Cards, Insurance, Rewards and Accounts.

Today, the Chqbook App offers over 60 products across Loans and Credit cards, offering Zero-fee and negotiated rates, all at one place. We also run a reward program for over 200,000 customers. Chqbook’s custom product programs don’t depend on traditional underwriting principles - we use alternate data and work with credit bureaus, partners and financial institutions to deliver tailor made products & services.

Awards: Winner of many prestigious awards like Fintech Rocketship Award 2019 under the UK-India Fintech Rocketship Programme, Tech30 at TechSparks 2019, and 2018 SuperStartups Asia Awards.
