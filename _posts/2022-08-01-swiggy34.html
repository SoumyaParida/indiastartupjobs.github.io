ABOUT THE TEAM & ROLE:

The All Things Supply Team at Swiggy works on various problem statements around-Vendor/Restaurant side of the Business, Kitchen Technology, Private Labels, Cloud Kitchen, Vendor Payouts, Trust and Safety etc. As Swiggy is venturing into new domains, we are looking for enthusiastic Engineers to join us in our mission to take India’s largest food delivery platform to new heights and be a part of a team that is building these new business lines from scratch

Dineout team is responsible for building this new business line from scratch and scaling the business from 1 to 100x while providing a “wow” customer experience. The team aims to solve building very rich and immersive experiences while ensuring low latency, high availability and scalability. The team will build and double down on building a roadmap that will enable Swiggy to deliver unparalleled convenience to customers while Dining out.

The Swiggy Dineout team is looking for engineers who are passionate about solving challenging problems, building systems that are high performance, highly scalable and available and provide “wow” experience to customers while having a lot of fun and learning.

What qualities are we looking for?

  • BTech/MTech in Computer Science or equivalent from a premier institute
  • Should have worked on developing automation tools or frameworks to test Big Data, Rest APIs, microservices, data migration, failover mechanism, blue green setups
  • Good knowledge on the DevOps tooling and processes.
  • Excellent understanding on the REST APIs and its usage.
  • Excellent Knowledge of SQL and/or Hive query scripting.
  • Experience in working or testing with distributed architecture (eg. Aerospike, DynamoDB, Elastic Search, Hadoop, Cassandra, Kafka, Data lake, etc..)
  • Experience with Amazon AWS or similar cloud providers
  • Ability to build Performance frameworks from ground up.
  • Experience in industry best practices in SW development processes: Unit testing, code coverage, OO design, code quality, automation, code reviews etc.
  • Good coding skills and debugging skills.
  • Problem solving capabilities, and solution provider.
  • Solving complex testing challenges and translate the same to the team.
  • Ability to collaborate and work well with others at all levels in the organization
  • Must be self-directed and detail oriented.

What will you get to do here?

  • Mentor and lead a large team of talented Software Development Engineers in Test
  • Define and monitor quality assurance metrics for continuous quality & process improvements
  • Focussed on Automation and continuously work on removing manual dependencies from the systems, innovate new methods to optimize testing cycles.
  • Creation of central quality tools and frameworks for better optimization of deliverables.
  • Estimating efforts, identify risks, devise mitigation and meet project schedules
  • Communicating clearly and openly with internal and external stakeholders regarding progress, roadblocks, and timelines.
  • Leading by example and keeping enthusiasm high in tight-deadline situations.
  • Oversee technical scoping and design of projects to ensure that projects deliver the expected value on time and with high quality
  • Work closely with management to identify risks, solve problems, and maintain progress and target online deliveries with high quality benchmarks.
  • Provide management with reports/metrics to display transparency into testing progress, automation summaries, and release readiness
  • Research and stay current on Quality Engineering Testing trends as the industry evolves.
