udaan is a B2B ecommerce platform that brings traders, wholesalers, distributors, retailers, and manufacturers in India on to a single platform. Our goal is to make ‘doing business’ easy in India & make B2B commerce convenient and efficient.

udaan Capital is the NBFC arm of Udaan & offers credit services to buyers and sellers on and off udaan’s B2B ecommerce platform to grow their business further. This space is largely untapped with less than 10% of MSMEs having access to working capital loans from financial institutions. In this way, udaan is uniquely positioned to formalize &scale trade credit.

Learn more about us: https://udaan.com/

Role Description

Function: Credit

Sub Function: Seller Side

Location: Bangalore

Reports to: Head Underwriting

Travel Frequency: Minimal

Department Hierarchy

Credit Policy

Experience range

1 year - 3 years

Job Responsibilities

  • Deciding trade credit for buyers (lending amount & period)
  • Responsible completion of timely credit appraisals
  • Adhering to strict TAT for underwriting files
  • Maintaining quality of underwriting as per SOP
  • Recommending deviation cases to the higher level or approving authority as per delegation matrix
  • Vendor Management

KRAs of the role

  • Delinquency Management – Maintain lower NPAs
  • TAT Management – Strict TAT to be maintained for underwriting files
  • Managing Quality of Processing – to maintain quality of underwriting adhering to SOP
  • Vendor Management – coordinate with vendors for getting various verification done for customers

Functional Competencies

  • Problem Solving: Resolution of overdue accounts by coordinating with a sales team. Coordination with Operations team for due/ overdue PDD process
  • Stakeholder Management: Meet Customers to understand them better for better credit appraisal
  • Cross-functional integration: Coordinate with sales and operations team for efficient functioning of the entire process

Candidate Profile

  • Qualification: CA/MBA Finance/B. Com
  • Must have a minimum 1 - 3 years of work experience in SME underwriting for products like Invoice Finance, Business Loans; prior experience in Fintech/NBFCs preferred
  • Thorough understanding of SME Financing and Credit processes
  • Good at market intelligence, should be able to provide inputs & feedback for policy and process improvement
  • Eye for detail/Diligent Personality
