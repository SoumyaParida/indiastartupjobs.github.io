Roles and Responsibilities

Relationship Manager: To source business proposal for Supply Chain Finance in assigned geography

New Business Development & Customer Relationship Management

  • Generate business leads in the assigned geography through multiple methods - cold calling, client visits, seminar, trade fair visits, existing relationships, references, online through Linkedin, etc. within the empaneled Anchor eco-system.
  • To follow-up on leads and source proposals from identified business prospects in the market.
  • Understand customer finance requirements & accordingly advice & provide customized solutions
  • Maintain the customer relations through timely servicing
  • Ensure greater share of business with the customer and references for new business leads

Operations & Monitoring

  • Coordinate for pre and post sanction process to ensure timely sanction of proposal, execution of legal documents and disbursement by liaising with credit, legal, compliance and operations team
  • Enable interaction of credit team with customer to aid proposal sanctioning.
  • Monitor regular client performance and report any significant development to superiors
  • Ensure timely collection of payments.
  • Monitor portfolio and submission of periodic reports to superiors
  • Ensure all compliances are maintained
  • Take adequate action to solve delinquent customers such as legal support, Market Information, Anchor information etc

Desired Skills and Experience

  • MBA Finance / CA

  • Ability to structure, discuss the deal contours with the relevant stakeholders – internal and external, and execute the transactions to meet the client's complex financing requirements (end to end) within the purview of business and regulatory requirements.
  • Be personable and have a presence; and be able to hold conversations and negotiations.

  • Ambitious and Ability to work in a fast-paced dynamic environment
