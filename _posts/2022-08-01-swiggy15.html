ABOUT THE TEAM AND THE ROLE

The All Things Supply Team at Swiggy works on various problem statements around-Vendor/Restaurant side of the Business, Kitchen Technology, Private Labels, Cloud Kitchen, Vendor Payouts, Trust and Safety etc. As Swiggy is venturing into new domains, we are looking for enthusiastic Engineers to join us in our mission to take India’s largest food delivery platform to new heights and be a part of a team that is building these new business lines from scratch.

Dineout team is responsible for building this new business line from scratch and scaling the business from 1 to 100x while providing a “wow” customer experience. The team aims to solve building very rich and immersive experiences while ensuring low latency, high availability and scalability. The team will build and double down on building a roadmap that will enable Swiggy to deliver unparalleled convenience to customers while Dining out.

The Swiggy Dineout team is looking for engineers who are passionate about solving challenging problems, building systems that are high performance, highly scalable and available and provide “wow” experience to customers while having a lot of fun and learning.

What Qualities are we looking for?

  • Technically Hands-on, with deep dive expertise in designing and developing real time internet applications or high scale SAAS applications
  • 4-7 years of experience in developing products with at least a couple of years in leading a team Excellent command over Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Exceptional coding skills in an Object-Oriented programming language (Java/ROR/Golang)
  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills
  • Experience with web technologies PHP/ Java, Python, Linux, Apache, MySQL, Solr, Memcache, Redis
  • Experience with high performance websites catering to millions of daily traffic is preferred

What kind of Projects will you work on ?

This role is for senior engineers to join the ATS (All Time Supply ) team where we build all technology and platforms related to the entire supply side of business at Swiggy (restaurants, stores etc.). This includes Order Relay Platforms, Kitchen Technology, Vendor Apps, Vendor Onboarding, Finance Engineering, Fraud Detection, Reporting. Some of the projects which you will be working on :

  • Design and Build a 4-way data driven reconciliation system for all money movements at Swiggy.
  • Develop an AR System to prevent money loss.
  • Build Vendor Payout , Credit systems and Ledger for Swiggy Businesses (Food, Grocery, Liquor, Dine Out etc)
  • Build Scalable and Accurate Money Movement and Transfers Platform.
  • Financial Data audit and Reporting.

  • Driving the adoption of best practices and regular Participation in code reviews, design reviews, architecture discussions.
  • Experiment with new and relevant technologies and tools, and drive adoption while measuring yourself on the impact you can create.

  • Decide technology and tool choices for your team and be responsible for them.
