We’re a grocery app devoted to delivering a wide range of everything fresh, and we’re on a very exciting path of redefining who we are and what we want to be. And you can be a part of this journey of learning and growing with us!

We’re looking to hire a Senior Copywriter for Supr Daily’s Creative Studio team! You are the right fit if you are…

A copywriter with 2-4 years of experience. (Bonus points if you’ve had stints in both an advertising agency and a brand.)

Who are we looking for?

Someone who is keen to:

  • Observe and understand how people behave, how homes work and what it means to Supr - mailers, print ads or campaigns, insights come first!
  • Think and research about every product we want to sell and what it means to our target audience. And of course, think brand-first!
  • Question every brief, line and creative. From “can the brief be better?” to “does this line align with our brand?”. How else do we get the best work out!
  • Experiment with approaches, insights, ideas in every asset they work on - no matter how big or small. (Push notifications are crucial too!)
  • Ideate for a range of requirements. We want you to be able to come up with lines and campaigns!
  • Write, backspace, rewrite! All to perfectly craft the story behind our products, while, of course, meeting timelines.
  • Refine creatives and communication with the help of performance-related data.
  • Build and own the crafting of brand tonality, treatment, voice, and its expression in marketing communication. And therefore, build Supr Daily!

What will your work look like?

You will get a chance to ideate and write on an entire range of requests, small and big.

Like - brand-led projects, launches, campaigns, events, and business as usual.

Assets include:

Videos, Postcards, Merchandising, Emailers, Push notifications, Print Ads & more!

If this sounds good to you, let’s talk!
