What you will do at Vymo

The Quality Engineering Function at Vymo is the champion of product quality. The QA Lead will be hands-on in all aspects of the Software Testing Life Cycle. He will act as a mentor /coach for the testing team and who has a passion for testing with the "break it" mentality.

A Day in your Life

  • You will be responsible for the design, development, and execution of test plan and strategies for black-box functional and non-functional testing
  • You will participate in product and feature design with developers, product managers, and business systems analysts.
  • You will develop plans, schedules, metrics, data needs, and scenarios for an end to end integration testing including areas such as database impacts, software scenarios, or usability
  • You will be analyzing the Test Cases prepared by all team members and communicate effectively with developers and other stakeholders and suggest potential fixes to bugs found during release testing.
  • You will own, test, and certify subsystems of the product.
  • You will work with R&D and QA in different geographical locations and time zones to diagnose, track, and manage quality issues to resolution.
  • You will work closely with the Engineering Manager for continuous process improvement and proactively gather feedback for continuous test improvement

What you have done:

  • Have a BE/MS/MCA in Computer Science or related discipline
  • Have 4-6 years of product testing experience particularly with large-scale enterprise products ( web and mobile)
  • Have experience in performing quality assurance testing including functional, compatibility, load balancing, and performance testing
  • Have established a solid relationship with Partners to understand the vision, requirements, and priorities of our business partners
  • Have detailed knowledge of SDLC and QA methodologies, concepts, processes, and documentation.
  • Have an in-depth understanding of IT software development life cycle and agile development environment like Scrum, Defect life cycle, application logic, Code Coverage tools, and Dev Code Debugging skills
  • Have experience in SELENIUM(JAVA), JMeter,Appium , eQA framework development experience, and Rest API based or UI
  • Have experience with testing either CRM software(Salesforce, Siebel, etc) or cloud technologies, Microservice architecture, High availability is a strong plus
  • Have hands-on experience on MySql and advanced DB technologies like document DBs (Mongo, Document DB)
  • Have the ability to learn and adapt quickly; ability to motivate people, instill accountability and achieve results on fast-paced projects
