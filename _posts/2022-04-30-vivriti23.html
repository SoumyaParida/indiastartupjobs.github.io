About the team and role

The risk team is responsible for overall risk framework and strategy for the organization. The team develops various risk models, arrives at various sector reports from a risk perspective and provides in depth analysis and recommendation around risk profiles of various clients.

As a member of the risk team you will be expected to possess an in depth understanding of the financial services sector, participate in due diligences with various clients along with the customer Acquisition team and eventually provide recommendation around the risk portfolio of each client.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze financial and business risk for Financial institutions and provide accurate update on their overall business model from a risk perspective.
  • Be brilliant with numbers, analysing financial statements, interpreting data and solving problems. Understand the balance sheet and analyse financial statements.
  • Participate in due-diligence and analysis, preparation of credit proposals, undertaking market research and presenting to the management
  • Experience in developing risk score card/risk score models would be an added advantage.
  • Conduct due diligence in various sectors like retail, manufacturing, Hospitality, Food and beverage.
  • Make sense of global markets and macros – economic analysis, drivers, what regulators do.
  • Assess the credit risks and suggest suitable risk mitigants.
  • Present and provide recommendations to credit committee around risk portfolio of the clients based on multiple aspects
  • Help stitch and execute a deal where necessary with the Business team.
  • Manage and influence internal as well as external stakeholders.

Desired Skills and Experience

  • Qualification : CA/MBA with specialization in Finance
  • Possess 2+ years of relevant experience in corporate / NBFC lending financial services client
  • Sector understanding in financial institution space.
  • Must have a track record of qualitative and quantitative credit analysis
  • Good knowledge of interpreting financial trends.
  • Strong systems thinking - ability to break down business objectives into executional projects, functional steps, and processes
  • Collaborative team player with great interpersonal skills and an exceptional ability to work effectively cross-functionally and with executive leadership
  • High attention to detail and excellent analytical skills
