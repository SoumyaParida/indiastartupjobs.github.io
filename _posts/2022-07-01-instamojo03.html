What you’ll do

This role is responsible for honing/validating go-to-market plan for Instamojo’s Ecommerce Partner Program. We are looking for someone who will be a critical stakeholder to grow the partner program & will help drive business by tapping into the agency ecosystem in India. The Channel Partner Manager will have strong channel and SaaS experience. This candidate should have strong deal and relationship management experience to grow and accelerate our partner GTM strategy & help drive pipeline growth. This role involves acquiring & managing partner relationships in India.

You will be the key advisor to these partners & be their go-to person. You will be responsible for their success. End of day, you will be the voice of these partners, to help Instamojo build & run a successful partner program.

Responsibilities

  • Passionate about the E-commerce industry, D2C brands & eager to power D2C growth in India amongst the MSME & SMB businesses.
  • Work closely with website developers, agencies & other partners who can add value to the Partner Program.
  • Understand their requirements, growth levers, business strategies to help define/drive feedback for the partner program’s go-to market strategy
  • Promote & drive partner acquisition cycles to add new partners
  • Drive Partner pipeline growth & ultimately revenue through partners.
  • Review & revise business plans based on partner needs/feedback.
  • Evaluate effectiveness of partner’s sales/services strategy & recommend improvements.
  • Address partner concerns & queries promptly & ensure partner satisfaction
  • Own end to end partner relationships

Qualifications

  • 5+ Years experience in Sales & Partner Management for a SaaS organization targeting MSME is a must.
  • Should have hands on experience in driving value addition with a partner program to MSME/SMB customers
  • Proven track record of growing channel partnerships with meaningful revenue impact
  • Ability to forecast channel revenue, and hands on experience on a CRM/PRM tool
  • Existing relationships in the ecommerce space, with marketing agencies, website developers, resellers & other VAS partners would be a plus.
