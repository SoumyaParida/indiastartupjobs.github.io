We need copy that reflects that ethos.

Copy that makes people feel something and causes them to act; not just click a link and be disappointed twenty seconds later.

Copy Maven @ Vymo

Copy is an integral part of the Growth function at Vymo. So much so that everyone writes copy.

But, as we scale it’s time for us to specialize. To that end, we’re hiring a copy maven who can help our prospects and customers realize how Vymo can transform their lives (and their businesses). With pithy and meaningful copy; none of that increase/decrease stuff!

What you will do:

  • Design brand and demand campaigns for US and Asia markets
  • Craft copy for campaigns, iterate based on results, and improve conversions
  • Work closely with Growth, Inside Sales, & Creative teams on Team OKRs
  • Build best practices and formulate the Vymo way of doing things for Copy

Some prerequisites:

  • Humility, curiosity and empathy to strive hard and learn our context
  • Phenomenal writing skills to craft compelling words and sentences
  • At least 4 years’ experience in B2B / SaaS (bonus if in Banking/Insurance)
  • Must have worked closely with Marketing, Sales, & Creative teams
