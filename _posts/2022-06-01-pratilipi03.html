Pratilipi Comics is a digital comics platform with over 9 lakh monthly readers. The Content team of Pratilipi Comics is responsible for sourcing, developing and delivering comics for the platform. And we are looking for Associate content editor. Candidate will work on delivering a variety of Original series on the platform.~

As an Associate content editor you will be

  • Responsible for the written content of the comic series that are assigned to you
  • Reading, curating, pitching and writing stories/series for developing original comics
  • Continuously learning, researching and developing insights on how to improve storytelling and writing of webtoons - webcomics

Who should apply

  • Anyone who has a knack of creative writing and storytelling
  • Anyone who wants to explore the world of webtoons and webcomics
