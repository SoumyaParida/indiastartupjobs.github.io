Zolve’s mission is simple: we want to make financial products accessible to everyone. Zolve offers the ambitious a plethora of products to simplify banking in the US, such as:

  • A Bank Account you can create from anywhere in the world in under five minutes
  • A high-limit Credit Card that helps build a healthy credit score
  • Easy, lightning-fast, cross-border money transfers with the best rates in the market

Zolve is expanding its horizons, and we’re excited to open our doors to everyone expanding theirs. We believe: that if space tourism is real, so is reaching for the stars.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Design best-in-class experiences for a global consumer base
  • Set design requirements based on information from internal teams, user research and benchmarking
  • Modify and revise existing designs to meet changing customer preferences.
  • Actively participate in user research activities to build a solid understanding of the problem space.
  • Build storyboards to conceptualize the experience and convey the design
  • Create wireframes, visual mock-ups and prototypes to communicate high-level design strategies and detailed interaction behaviors
  • Rapidly iterate to test your designs and design the user experience
  • Challenge and push teams to elevate the customer experience at each stage
  • Work closely with product, tech, and business to identify project goals and deliver high-quality work (user flows, wireframes, low/high fidelity UI mockups, prototypes...)
  • Take ownership and iteratively design through conceptual and development cycles
  • Identify new product improvement opportunities
  • Analyze how a new product satisfies market needs and consumer preferences.
  • Build, direct and mentor the team
  • Create detailed design specs and assets that makes engineering teams more effective in bringing the product to life
  • Follow and promote design best practices and work with the design team to curate, gate keep and develop the Zolve brand and design system

REQUIREMENTS

  • Portfolio showcasing best-in-class products, top notch interaction skills and meticulous attention to detail.
  • 10+ years of experience designing multi-platform product experiences in a fast-paced, agile development environment
  • Data driven: Can articulate and defend design decisions backed by research and data analysis.
  • Proficiency in standard design tools like Figma
  • Good communication skills: written and verbal
  • Experience working collaboratively with geographically dispersed teams
  • Good knowledge of frontend principles to make informed design decisions
  • Storytelling skills: showcasing your work, process, and decisions to cross-functional stakeholders
  • Previous experience designing global consumer products would be a plus
  • Previous exposure to fintech domain/start-ups would be a plus
