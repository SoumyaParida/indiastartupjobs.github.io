Job Description

We're a small team that's growing fast and we're looking for a Head - UI/UX Designer who will work closely with product managers and key stakeholders to help discover product requirements while translating customer needs into great user experiences and enabling our products to be highly attractive and functional. If you believe that you drive for excellence in what you do and would go an extra mile to achieve that, this might be an interesting opportunity for you.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to execute creative solutions.
  • Handling a team of 5-7 designers
  • Design intuitive interfaces and craft authentic experiences with business goals in sight.
  • Brings ideas to the table and explain his/her point of view and take/give feedback and direction.
  • Balances multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines without breaking a sweat.
  • Has a firm understanding of information architecture and UX design.
  • Sketches and wireframes digital experiences.
  • Creates user flows, workflow mappings, and other communication collaterals.
  • Conduct research, user study, and usability testing.
  • Delivers clear information architecture in all deliverables
  • Design consistent interfaces and experiences across multiple devices.
  • Works closely with stakeholders to design new features from the ground up.

Job Requirement

Minimum Qualifications:

5+ years of experience as a Lead - UX/UI designer. A strong product design portfolio that demonstrates how they solved for both business requirements and customer needs. Preferably someone who has delivered successful designs for responsive web, iOS, and Android platforms.

Required Qualifications:

Excellent interaction skills with the ability to communicate and present his/her work clearly. Readily gives and receives constructive feedback. Some experience using tools such as Sketch, Invision, Adobe XD, Figma, and Zeplin.
