Job Description

  • Design and develop new, end-to-end financial product experiences in the BFSI domain for retail consumers.
  • Work cross-functionally with Engineering teams across multiple product teams to drive buy-in on new product experiences and engineering frameworks.
  • Work closely with your and other engineering teams with a keen eye towards scalable architecture to design and implement new 0→1 integrations with an appetite to quickly scale from 1→n across products
  • Recruit and build an entirely new engineering team and move fast in converting new ideas to product collaborating with Product, Design, Leadership, and other functions, while maintaining high product quality and reliability.
  • Staying ahead of the curve by lending a stable structure to the teams and products while bringing operational excellence as products mature.

Job Requirement

  • Above 9 years of experience with technical depth as well as good people skills.
  • Exceptional design and architectural skills
  • Expertise and interest in growing teams-interviewing junior and senior engineers, collaborating with a recruiting team, smoothly onboarding new team members
  • Excellent leadership skills to mentor the engineers under you.
  • Deep understanding of technologies and architecture in a highly scalable and available set-up.
  • Superior project management skills to manage multi-engineer projects and experience in delivering high-quality projects on time.
  • Have experience guiding the continuous improvement of processes and technology.
  • Be able to communicate clearly and consistently with teams.
  • Have spent 2+ years at a start-up or worked in a small and reasonably independent team/dev center (Good to have)
  • BE/BTech or some equivalent academic stance.
