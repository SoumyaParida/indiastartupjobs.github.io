What are we looking for :

(A) Overall Metrics Responsibility

  • Gross Margin Profile - Need to analyse margins for every customer month on month across categories, geographies, content type, customer type, and industry
  • NRR and GRR - Create detailed customer segmentation and drive cohorts’ expansion with focus on both Revenue expansion and Churn Mapping
  • Recurring Revenue – Benchmarking each customer acquisition based on Recurring revenue and driving quality and predictability in revenue growth
  • Contribution Margins and EBITDA margins - Identify what are the key drivers and cost-centres / what does our contribution margin profile look like and how do we drive bottom line improvement

(B) Budgeting and Forecasting (AOP)

  • Cost Forecasting on a monthly level and doing a detailed analysis across cost optimization,subscriptions
  • Revenue Predictability and projections and determine the predictability of revenue monthly, quarterly and annually
  • Giving directional sense on how the P&L is shaping up vis-à-vis our initial goals

(C) P&L Management, FP&A & Business reviews

  • Identifying leading and lagging metrics and accordingly working with the business leaders to drive performance and accountability
  • Setting up a Reporting framework on metrics that impact P&L and indicate the health of the business across multiple splices
  • Analysis to be presented to the board across board meetings / investor requests
  • Establish ROI on marketing investments, with the right assessment of risks and opportunities
  • Conducting business case analysis of new programs and services
  • Burn Optimisation
  • Guide the team on rigorous data-based analysis for any decision-making scenario, bringing out business insights not visible in regular reviews
  • Participate in monthly closing calls with the controller’s organization for P&L Review

((D) Special Projects
