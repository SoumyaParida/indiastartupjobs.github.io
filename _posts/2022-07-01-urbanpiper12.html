UrbanPiper is the leading SaaS platform in the restaurant industry. We’re helping the smallest of restaurants to the largest chains across the world to grow their in-store and online commerce. Right from automating all their workflows across online delivery platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Deliveroo, UberEats—to building and deploying self-branded websites and apps, right to managing their walk-in customers and dine-in experiences, UrbanPiper is the preferred choice for over 20k+ restaurants.

We are backed by top VCs — Tiger Global and Sequoia Capital. Recently, we closed our Series B round of funding with Swiggy and Zomato also participating together! That’s a first and only (until now) event for any startup in the food and beverage industry.

The team:

The Biz-Ops team is the central nervous system of the organization which consumes data across all functions (sales, marketing, finance, operations) and lays the groundwork for data backed decision making. The two key pillars on which the Biz-Ops team has been built are "Efficiency" and "Predictability". The team's responsibility includes building tools to collect data, using the tech database to auto fetch data, driving adoption with functions to ensure correct data is input, and building automation for invoicing, reporting, and decision making.

Your role:

As a Senior Data Analyst, you will be responsible to build dashboards which show key metrics of different functions and automate the dashboards such that the data is represented real time. You will have to build processes to ensure that data is flowing in the correct manner, put data structures in place to ensure all data can be automatically updated in a regular manner. The ownership and accountability of all data being presented will lie with the role.

What you will do:

  • Build a comprehensive automated system for invoicing customers across multiple countries using product data from tech databases, agreement data from finance/sales.
  • Build a scalable finance database to store all data used for invoicing
  • Identify the gaps in the invoicing process to ensure all customers are accurately invoiced every month
  • Build dashboards and project/function specific reports to report business performance at a company level (New customers, churn, revenue and transaction level cohorts etc.)
  • Streamline the data for onboarding, support and other delivery functions and build dashboards which can be used for data backed decision making
  • Report on financial performance and prepare for regular leadership reviews
  • Conceptualize and drive projects using data available to drive higher revenue/improve efficiency

We are looking for someone who has/is (must have):

  • 4+ years of experience in data analytics
  • Ability to understand project requirements, provide inputs, gather requirements, plan project roadmap and drive it to execution.
  • Proficiency in Excel & google spreadsheets, SQL and data visualization tools such as Tableau, PowerBI, Aws quick sight, etc.,
  • Superior problem-solving abilities and strong analytical thinking
  • Desire to work in a fast-paced, challenging environment where you need to push yourself all the time
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal; ability to articulate the problems and roadblocks to relevant stakeholders within the company
  • Solution orientation and self-drive
  • Ability to create deliverables in Excel and PowerPoint, Optimization techniques (linear, non-linear), and knowledge of VBA, Excel Macro programming, Data Studio
  • Ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy.

Nice to haves:

  • Python/R
  • Modularized Coding, Object-Oriented Programming
  • Ability to explore and implement various statistical and analytical techniques like regression, supervised/unsupervised learning, forecasting, etc. for sales funneling
