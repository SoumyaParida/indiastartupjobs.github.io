Role Summary

Currently in the process of building our teams, we require a seasoned Engineering Manager with a background in Product Engineering and a well-rounded experience across various areas of design and architecture, technology roadmaps, technical mentoring, product delivery and more.

The ideal candidate will have roughly 8 to 12 years of relevant industry experience and a strong technology acumen coupled with excellent people management skills.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical leadership to major projects and guide engineering managers, along with their teams of 10-15 top notch engineers
  • Chalk-out and implement the vision, technology roadmap and business goals
  • Enable team contribution towards product strategies
  • Create clear career paths for team members and ensure their growth by mentoring them
  • Evaluate performances regularly and share and seek feedback
  • Operate with scale and speed, amidst flux in the fast-paced environment
  • Work closely with Engineering Leaders, Managers; Product Management, Business Development and Operation teams, and enable them by providing scalable and high quality technology solutions at a rapid pace
  • Set up best practices for development and overlook its adoption
  • Architect and design technically robust, flexible and scalable solutions
  • Work with designers, business analysts, and product managers to estimate and plan projects in an agile environment
  • Drive exceptional performance and collaborate with other teams on unclear interfaces amidst rapidly evolving organizational activities

Key Requirements:

  • BE/B.Tech (or equivalent degree) in Computer Science
  • Excellent leadership skills to manage and mentor teams
  • A deep understanding of technologies and architecture in a highly scalable and available set-up
  • Expertise in highly transactional, large relational and complex systems
  • Strong object oriented design skills, and an uncanny ability to design intuitive modules and class-level interfaces
  • Superior management skills to manage multi-engineer projects and deliver high quality projects on time
  • Strong technical competence with a track record of individual technical accomplishments

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
  • Opportunities in a growing and sustainable industry.
  • A chance to work with revolutionary technology and AI based solutions.
