Department

Mechanical Engineering

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Study the products in portfolio and analyze the features and determine the scope of improvement in the aspects of Engineering and User Handling Experience
  • Understanding the product requirements and coming up with conceptual design of the product
  • Detailed engineering design of Concepts, Components and Mechanisms
  • Work with Industrial Design Team and core engineering team to develop concepts and carry out the development
  • Perform various Aggregate calculations and selection of components, testing and validation of those components
  • Creation of CAD Part and Assembly Models, Part and Assembly Drawings
  • Design and development of toolings related to components and assemblies
  • Work with Vendors and Suppliers to develop the components as per specifications

Requirements

  • Experience in Product Engineering of Consumer Electronics/Domestic Appliances/Automotive Interior Mechanisms/Medical Devices
  • Expert and Experience in Mechanisms Design and Electromechanical components
  • Strong knowledge and experience of working with Polymers and Metals
  • Expert at Sheet Metal Design and Fabrication processes for press tools
  • Proficient in tooling and manufacturing through Injection and Blow molding
  • Strong CAD tool expertise, including surface design
  • Good at creation of Part and Assembly drawings with Tolerances and other attributes

Good to Have:

  • Experience of handling end to end Product Design
  • Ability to coordinate with all internal and external stakeholders to drive the product development phase
  • Knowledge of regulatory and safety standards of industrial machinery
  • Mass Production components development experience
  • Certified Courses in Design Thinking

Eligible candidates:

  • Work Experience: 3-5 years of experience in relevant domain

We Value:
