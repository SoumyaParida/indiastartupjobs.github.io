This role is for an experienced professional, willing to take a seat on the rocket ship that has already launched. We are an e-commerce brand acquirer, and all our operations are focused on marketplace first businesses. The candidate selected for this role will work to ensure our company’s portfolio brands are growing aggressively and out do industry benchmarks and achieve what we would call explosive growth. This is a demanding role but since you are still reading this job description, we have concluded you love a good challenge!

As a Sourcing specialist you will be responsible for handling the company’s end to end sourcing operations. We are looking for a highly motivated sourcing specialist who will appraise and improve our sourcing activities by analyzing the company’s spending, supplying partnerships and new possibilities.

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for sourcing goods, materials, and services at competitive prices for 10club - Baby & Toys, Sports & Fitness and Home Garden portfolio.
  • Analyzing and calculating costs of procurement and suggest methods to decrease expenditure.
  • Know-how of import/domestic sourcing and implement inventory optimization strategies within the company.
  • Discover profitable suppliers from across the key souring locations.
  • Maintain and update the supplier database and find alternative suppliers if the existing ones are unable to meet their obligations.
  • Work with growth team and other stakeholders to manage and execute new product developments.
  • Establishing standards with regards to quality, delivery, and finalize purchase details of orders and deliveries.
  • Researching and keeping abreast with the latest market trend.
  • Negotiating price, quantity, and delivery schedules with suppliers.
  • Cooperate with stakeholders to guarantee agreement on terms and processes.

Requirements:

  • 2-7 years of experience in sourcing processes.
  • BE/B.Tech /MBA/PGDM would be preferred.
  • Good with figures and in collecting, analysing and interpreting data.
  • Data-oriented approach to problem-solving.
  • Strong negotiation skills.
  • Sound time-management and organizational skills.
