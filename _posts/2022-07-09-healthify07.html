Group Company:HealthifyMe Wellness Private Limited

Designation:Associate Director - Data Science and Analytics

Office Location:Bangalore (India)

Position description:

Data is central to HealthifyMe’s decision-making process, and both - business intelligence & data science are critical components of our engine - to evangelize data-driven decision making across product management, business strategy and operations.

As an Analytics Leader at HealthifyMe, you would call the shots on all of the above with the help of a talented analytics team that currently runs the show for HealthifyMe. Your guidance will help in shaping the way product performance is measured, defining the questions that need to be asked, and scaling analytics methods and tools to support our growing business, leading the way for high quality, high velocity decisions for your team and the entire organization.

For this role, we’re looking for an experienced product leader who will help HealthifyMe scale across geographies, launch new features catered to both developed and developing markets, And become the world’s leading Health and Wellness Platform.

You will be responsible to define and track KPIs, support and lead strategic initiatives, design experiment frameworks, evaluate A/B tests, implement data instrumentation, and run the brain of the organization with the help of your team.

If you thrive in a fast paced environment that is filled with ambiguity, like working with humble-yet-smart people, enjoy data led conversations across all domains - this is the role for you!

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

You would be expected to:

  • Be the central point of contact for all of business, product, marketing and operations leadership members
  • Gather requirements and create your own requirements based on business and user needs
  • Design short and long term roadmaps for our capable analytics team
  • Guide the team to execute with precision, quality and pace
  • Take decisions/Recommend decisions depending on the size of the problem and work alongside all other teams in HealthifyMe to see initiatives through
  • Ensure that all stakeholders in the organization are made independent of the analytics team’s help - by streamlining daily/weekly reporting of metrics
  • Design and build a better data infrastructure to be scale ready for international expansion
  • Most importantly, scale the current analytics/data science team to be future ready
