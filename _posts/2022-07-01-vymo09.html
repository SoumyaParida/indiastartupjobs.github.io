What you will do

As an Engineering Manager, you will own the application and product development and provide scalable and high-quality technology solutions at a rapid pace. This is a technical hands-on management position which is a player-coach role and encompasses people management, technical/ functional expertise, and relationship/ stakeholder management. We are looking at an engineering leader who believes in Impact creation at scale and is effective at influencing a culture of engineering craftsmanship and excellence.
