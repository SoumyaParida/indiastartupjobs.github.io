About Miko:

Miko is a global deep tech company with offices in US, UK and India. The company focusses on bringing Emotionally Intelligent product offerings to consumers across the globe to solve unmet societal needs. The first part of the company’s vision has manifested into an emotionally intelligent companion robot for kids. Miko’s products are available as a "content on subscription" hardware platform powered by its own patented technologies.

Founded in 2015, Miko is company passionate about bringing the best of technology to young learners everywhere. Home to the world’s leading educators, engineers, psychologists, and content developers, Miko takes learning to a whole new level through state-of-the-art artificial intelligence, robotics and IoT. The company has a global mindset and presence, with 300+ employees, offices in the innovation hubs of Silicon Valley and Mumbai, and customers across 140+ countries.

About the Position:

This is an exciting opportunity to work at the cross-section of Fund Raising, Investing and M&A at Miko. As a M&A Associate, you will be partnering with the Business Areas to drive Fund raising, alongside identifying and developing a pipeline of target companies that will shape Miko’s product portfolio.

The candidate will utilize industry best practices in fund raising, identify and successfully execute mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, and related activities. As part of Corporate Strategy and Development, the candidate will be critical in helping the corporation realize its strategic objectives through inorganic growth.

In this role, the candidate will develop strong cross functional skills by working closely with finance, business operations, IT, HR, and Legal. They will develop a streamlined roadmap to supporting the overall success of the investment. The M&A Associate will also work on post-investment activities such as business performance monitoring, claims, reps/warranty monitoring, etc. They will develop a post-merger economic analysis to evaluate and improve value capture within Corporate Development.

Responsibilities

  • Researching industry trends and competition which will help in identifying areas of focus for strategic investments and acquisitions
  • Evaluating potential acquisition targets - both qualitatively and quantitatively
  • Preparing investment memos/ target profiles for the senior management- Supporting the entire deal cycle - from sourcing till closing, including integration
  • Supporting fundraising and other strategic activities- Support with the internal and external stakeholders' co-ordination; Internal stakeholders would include BU heads & support functions and external stakeholders would include investment bankers, diligence agencies, consultants etc.
  • As an integral part of M&A team, the candidate will be actively involved in assisting with corporate transactions like M&A, JV, capital raising etc.
  • To actively assist in all process elements of M&A function including but not limited to market intelligence, deal solicitation, diligence and deal completion
  • Assist the team in creation of reports such as external intelligence on Industry/ Competition/ business environment to identify opportunities for growth and possible challenges for business
  • Assist the team with Financial and legal Due Diligence.
  • Assist the Team with creation of pitch decks for Board and Investor Meetings
  • Building detailed business case decks including detailed target profile, synergy projections, shareholder value creation, etc.
  • Providing legal support for all related transactions; working closely with cross-functional teams and specialists in other groups across internal and external teams
  • Reviewing documents for legal due diligence in parallel to or independent of external law firms
  • Coordination with external law firms/financial consultants and provision of internal advice on various syndicated, structured and project financing deals

Skills required:

  • Financial/Business Modelling, Analysis, Market Research, Valuation Techniques, Transaction documents’ review
  • Planning and developing creative investment monitoring strategies and approaches to realize business objectives and maximize value creation
  • Well organized, with strong management skills and be able to get the best from colleagues to ensure successful delivery to deadlines
  • Cross functional and cross business area experience
  • Clear understanding of corporate strategy initiatives
  • Strong communication, presentation, and facilitation skills

Qualification:

  • MBA + Engineer, with 2+ years of post MBA experience
  • Prior experience in Fund Raising, Corporate M&A, Investment banking, Investment Research, Venture Capital will be a plus
  • Strong corporate finance, analytical and communication skills required
