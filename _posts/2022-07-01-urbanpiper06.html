UrbanPiper offers an integration layer that powers the operational efficiency of 1000’s of restaurants across multiple countries for selling online through their own white-labeled platform or any of the leading food aggregator platforms — Swiggy, Ubereats, Zomato, Deliveroo, JustEat, Talabat, etc. The most defining aspects of our work are related to — consistency, reliability, scalability, and agility. We make use of a wide variety of technologies and process significantly large amounts of data on a daily basis.

We are looking for server application developers who have an appetite to explore new technologies, while building applications that can scale well both in terms of execution speed and the size of data that is processed.

You will:

  • Work closely with the product/project management team to understand the scope of an application
  • Be expected to research technologies that provide ideal solutions
  • Need to have a good grasp of how to design and implement APIs in conformance to the specifications of REST. • Need to have a good understanding of network principles related to latency, throughput, bandwidth, security — and how to monitor them.
  • Take ownership of the correctness of your work both in terms of functionality and usability aspects
  • Apply the best practices of software development when it comes to SCM, managing issues, and most importantly, documenting your work.

We are looking for someone who has:

  • 5+ years of experience in building integration solutions with REST APIs, DB, Email, SMS, etc.
  • A sound understanding of disparate persistence SQL and NoSQL storage systems (both document and columnar stores).
  • Worked with Python on a regular basis and is conversant with the dominant frameworks like Django, Flask, and those required to implement RESTful protocols.
  • A strong understanding of data structures.
  • Conversant with the AWS platform (or an equivalent IaaS/PaaS) and have a good understanding of working remotely on servers using SSH and CLI.
  • The ability to communicate results clearly in a verbal and written manner.
  • Can install and operate infrastructure applications that can be used to monitor performance, alert and recover.

Good to have:

  • Experience in a high-growth technology startup company.
  • Ability to use a UI/UX tool like Figma or Sketch to design interfaces and workflows.
  • Familiar with the concepts of distributed systems esp. Queues
