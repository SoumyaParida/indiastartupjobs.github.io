About Control Tower:

The control tower is a team that identifies operational issues using real time data to highlight the potential breach and fix these issues by coordinating with operations team. The function includes follow ups on calls until the issue is resolved. A root cause analysis on the recurring issues and provide the solution on the same is also the responsibility of control tower.
