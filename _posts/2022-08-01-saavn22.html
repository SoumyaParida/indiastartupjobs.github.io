ROLE:

We're looking for a Assistant Manager – Financial Reporting and Consolidation to be a key member of our growing Finance team. This role would report to the AD - Finance and will work closely with the internal and external customers to achieve the compliance, business and financial goals of the organization. This person should have very good working experience in ERPs, Excel, USGAAP, IND-AS.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Hands-on experience of IND-AS (and US GAAP) reporting and consolidation of Indian and US subsidiaries. Involves periodic closing and financial reporting for both standalone and consolidated in line with group policies.
  • Ensuring all monthly close activities are completed by WD+2 for all India and US entities as per requirement and formats as mandated by Management, Investors and relevant stakeholders
  • Engage with cross functional teams to provide comprehensive analysis and comment on variances and trends in relation to monthly reporting and budgets/forecasts
  • Working closely with various stakeholders within the organisation and group to work on various ad hoc projects along with monthly routines.
  • Proactively being connected with the recent developments in INDAS/IFRS/US GAAP and undertaking impact assessment of any change in law, rules via notifications and circulars and disseminating the knowledge down to the Finance and Accounting team
  • Work closely with Group Tax Advisors, consultants as and when required for ensuring proper implementation of INDAS/USGAAP/IFRS and relevant accounting standards to the business and operations.
  • Above mentioned are the specific requirements from the candidature, however depending on the circumstances generic challenging assignments will be given.
  • Along with the routines, ability to add value by new initiatives, learning and development.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Chartered Accountant with 0-3 years post qualification experience with articleship experience from mid to large sized firm, involving assignments of public limited/listed companies.
  • Candidates with prior experience from internet/online industry would be preferred (nice to have but not must)
  • Hands on experience in Consolidation of Indian and US subsidiaries as per provisions of INDAS is a must.
  • Detailed knowledge of Indian GAAP/IND-AS/US GAAP/IFRS/ IFC (must have)
  • Ability and willingness for hands on working for Tally/ERP (must have)
  • Strong Spreadsheet/Excel skills (macros etc) (must have)
  • A strong working knowledge of technical accounting and business matters
  • The candidate should be in touch with the latest developments on Taxation and Accounting matters in India and worldwide.
  • Under IFC, knowledge of documentation and understanding internal checks and controls and processes, identifying weaknesses and suggesting and implementing procedures for the same.
  • Conducting routine and extensive testing of controls to identify gaps and make changes where needed.

Soft Skills:

  • Ability to act and operate independently with minimal daily direction from manager to accomplish objectives
  • Excellent communication skills with capability to highlight critical issues with impact and provide solutions within and outside the Finance team
  • Strong analytical, decision-making and problem solving skills and abilities
  • Self-initiator, pro-active and result driven attitude
  • Ability work under stringent deadlines along with multi-tasking
