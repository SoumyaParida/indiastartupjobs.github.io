About Swiggy:

Swiggy is the leading food and grocery ordering and delivery platform in India. It is Changing the Way India Eats by becoming the most reliable one-stop solution for consumer’s food and grocery needs. Swiggy's innovative technology, large and nimble delivery service and customer focus enable a host of benefits including lightning-fast deliveries, live order tracking, no restrictions on minimum order amounts, and strong customer support.

ABOUT THE TEAM :

The charter of this team is to build Location Intelligence products that help Swiggy build a better understanding of the real world and enable hyperlocal optimizations that significantly improve customer experience, growth and profitability for Swiggy. We are looking for strong, highly motivated, independent engineers and data scientists, who are excited about working with large scale geo-spatial data and AI (ML and Deep Learning) to enable Location Intelligence. The technology solutions built by the Location Intelligence Team are at the heart of Swiggy’s ability to deliver food to customers.

The team’s charter encompasses various aspects of hyperlocal Location Intelligence, built using our internal data. This includes points of interest, metadata for POIs, addresses, routes etc. With Swiggy’s unparalleled hyperlocal density, our Location Intelligence and Data Sciences team leverages the travel time/route data of delivery partners currently delivering orders, to predict accurate delivery distance and ETAs. High quality location intelligence is essential to help delivery partners navigate to the precise customer location and to provide live tracking of the order status and driver journey to customers. This ensures a great experience for customers and for delivery partners.
