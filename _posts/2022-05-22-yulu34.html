Role Summary

As Logistics Manager, you will be involved in organizing and monitoring distribution of vehicles by effectively communicating with various stakeholders and vendors. You will also be responsible for handling a team and coordinating with them for the smooth flow of relocation processes.In addition to having excellent organizational skills, you should also possess in-depth knowledge of logistics and inventory systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Responsible for enhancing revenue per trips, streamlining workflow, evolving effective operational procedures involving City Logistics/Relocation
  • Accountable for reliability and overall bike availability.
  • Responsible for work along with the control room team, monitor daily escalations/ SLAs, conduct Daily, Weekly & Monthly review meetings with internal stakeholders and truck vendors.
  • Identify new potential truck vendors.
  • Work cohesively with design & operations teams for driving process efficiency and continuously optimize cost and improve vehicle utilization.
  • Work closely with product/tech and operations teams for new product development, implementation, adoption in addition to solve for tech challenges.

Key Requirements:

  • 5 to 7 years experience into logistics management
  • Be a self-starter who thrives in a rapidly growing organization. Agility and ability to adapt quickly to changing requirements and scope and priorities.
  • Great communication and interpersonal skills.
  • A data-driven decision-making approach with ability to deal with ambiguity in the absence of data.
  • Process oriented mindset and system oriented mindset ; A go-getter with an execution-oriented mindset who is excited about making a big impact. Understanding of the domain, i.e. operations design, logistics, supply chain, fleet management and mobility will be an added advantage

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
