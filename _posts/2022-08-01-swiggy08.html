ABOUT THE TEAM & ROLE:

The Marketplace Fulfillment Team is responsible for the entire post order experience at Swiggy. The team consists of Core Logistics, Driver experience and solutions to enable and power our Customer Service agents with the Agent Workbench, Chatbots and Live Tracking screen for Customers and Agents.

Serviceability within MFP focuses on when to take orders (and when not) based on available supply of drivers, estimated time to deliver etc. It is a customer backwards construct - it endeavors to deliver the best customer experience under the constraints of driver supply, weather/traffic conditions etc. It also makes a “promise” to customers (time to deliver) while making optimal trade-off decisions between “number of orders to accept” and “confidence to deliver within promised SLA”.

What you will be doing

The successful candidate will have influence over the vision, features and design of the critical data used to drive the business.
