Right from automating all their workflows across online delivery platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Deliveroo, UberEats—to building and deploying self-branded websites and apps, right to managing their walk-in customers and dine-in experiences, UrbanPiper is the preferred choice for over 20k+ restaurants.

The team:

The Enterprise Sales team is the revenue generation engine at UrbanPiper. Their superpower lies in prospecting and engaging large restaurant chains that are looking to improve their revenue generation capability and operational efficiency.

Your role:

As National BD Head for the Enterprise segment, you will lead a team of Business Development Managers focussed on large enterprise chains (>20 locations). You will acquire new customers and build meaningful relationships to convert them into happy UrbanPiper clients by helping them realize the value in adopting one or more of our product offerings.

We are looking for a proactive leader who has demonstrated success dealing with ambiguity, operating in a high-growth environment, managing high-performing teams and solving problems with limited oversight. Ideal candidates will have experience scaling and managing a technology sales team, and possess first-class analytical, communication and relationship-building skills.

We’re looking for entrepreneurial leaders who are passionate about building a team and growing a business from the ground up.

You will:

  • Develop the long-term vision and strategy for the India Enterprise segment and prepare the staffing plan.
  • Recruit, develop and lead a team of BDMs that understand the sales process and are great at building customer relationships.
  • Assume shared responsibility with the on-boarding team to ensure a smooth go-live experience for customers.
  • Manage day-to-day operations using CRM tools (Salesforce), with a focus on performance against key sales metrics.
  • Engage all stakeholders in the customer ecosystem and the key decision makers by visiting them in person or engaging them over calls to get them to demo our product offering and convert into paying customers.
  • Coach and guide the team in developing consultative and solution-based sales skills through all facets of the sales cycle.
  • Play a personal and direct role in building and maintaining enduring customer relationships, up to the C-level.
  • Inspire, motivate and enable personal and career development of team members, both to promote career growth and to increase impact for UrbanPiper.
  • Work collaboratively and cross-functionally with product, sales engineering, marketing and customer success teams in the organization to help shape UrbanPiper’s products and solutions to meet customer needs.
  • Participate in industry events to help drive market awareness & win over advocates.
  • Own India Enterprise revenue goals and manage against aggressive sales targets.
  • Collaborate closely with Sales / Revenue Operations to consistently improve the sales process through standardization and best practice adoption.

We are looking for someone who has/is:

  • 10+ years of experience in Sales and in a leadership role for the 4+ years.
  • Good understanding of the food tech industry. Ability to understand and articulate technical concepts, and translate them into industry- or business-relevant terms.
  • Ability to hire, train and coach a high-performance sales team.
  • Capable of exhibiting an empathetic, precise and articulate approach towards engaging senior decision makers and helping them understand the business value in adopting a SaaS/technical solution.
  • A clear and correct manner of communicating their thoughts verbally or in a written form.
  • Ability to operate in an ambiguous and fast-paced environment.
  • Experience at selling a SaaS solution and able to understand technical topics comfortably.
  • Well versed in managing their day-to-day activities on a CRM tool like Salesforce.
  • Hands on leader - inherent urge to spend time with customers; cannot sit behind a desk.
