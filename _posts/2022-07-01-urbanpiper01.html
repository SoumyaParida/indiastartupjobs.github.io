Description

UrbanPiper is the leading SaaS platform in the restaurant industry. We’re helping the smallest of restaurants to the largest chains across the world to grow their in-store and online commerce. Right from automating all their workflows across online delivery platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Deliveroo, UberEats—to building and deploying self-branded websites and apps, right to managing their walk-in customers and dine-in experiences, UrbanPiper is the preferred choice for over 20k+ restaurants.

We are backed by top VCs — Tiger Global and Sequoia Capital. Recently, we closed our Series B round of funding with Swiggy and Zomato also participating together! That’s a first and only (until now) event for any startup in the food and beverage industry.

The team:

The Sales team is the revenue generation engine at UrbanPiper. Their superpower lies in prospecting and engaging restaurant chains that are looking to improve their revenue generation capability and operational efficiency.

Your role:

As an Account Executive, you will be expected to build meaningful relationships with prospective customers and convert them into happy UrbanPiper clients by helping them realise the value in adopting one or more of our product offerings.

You will:

  • Own the full sales cycle from lead to closure for enterprise restaurant chains (>20 locations).
  • Identify and work on a portfolio of enterprise restaurant chains that are not using our products.
  • Develop CxO relationships at all levels.
  • Develop relationships with key decision makers and get them to demo our product offering and convert into paying customers.
  • Experience managing complex RFP responses for new and existing customers.
  • Lead and contribute to team projects to develop and refine our products and the sales process.
  • Keep meticulous notes to track our engagement with prospective customers, using CRM and other sales tools.
  • Be responsible for personal target compliance, reliable revenue forecasting and ownership of sales pipeline as communicated through CRM.
  • Assume shared responsibility with the on-boarding team to ensure a smooth go-live experience for customers.
  • Ensure timely and accurate billing for existing customers.
  • Identify and confirm customer's needs and business strategies by performing competitive analysis to identify and drive business partnerships.

We are looking for someone who has/is:

  • 2-5 years of experience in a role involving sales or BD to enterprise customers.
  • A good understanding of the food tech industry.
  • Capable of exhibiting an empathetic, precise and articulate approach towards engaging senior decision makers and helping them understand the business value in adopting a SaaS/technical solution.
  • A clear and correct manner of communicating their thoughts verbally or in a written form.
  • Ability to operate in an ambiguous and fast-paced environment.
  • Experience at selling a SaaS solution and able to understand technical topics comfortably.
  • Well versed in managing their day-to-day activities on a CRM tool like Salesforce.
  • Good analytical and quantitative capabilities.

Nice to have:

  • Prior experience in a high growth startup environment.
  • Experience in the F&B industry.
  • Feels at home working with spreadsheets for tracking and analytical tasks.
  • Your own mode of transportation.
