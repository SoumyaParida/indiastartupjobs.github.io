Job Description

Bangalore. Candidates will be responsible for a wide range of duties related to investigating and eliminating online e-commerce risk. Ideal applicants will have experience in the e-commerce payments space, previous trust and safety experience, and experience succeeding in a customer-driven workplace. All candidates will be analytical and capable of succeeding in a fast-paced team environment. The Risk Investigator position relies on excellent judgment to plan and accomplish goals and has to be a self-starter. Excellent individual problem-solving and analytical skills are used to authenticate customers and complex transactions. Nearly all decisions are expected to be made independently with minimal oversight and a high degree of accuracy.

The Risk Investigator will be required to engage in frequent written and verbal communication with department management, the Tech Team, and top management. They may also be required to contact customers by phone and/or email.

Productivity and quality assurance will be evaluated along with the overall contribution to the development of the department.
