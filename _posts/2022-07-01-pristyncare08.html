About Us :-

Brief about Pristyn Care :

Pristyn Care is a Series-E Sequoia & Tiger global-funded Healthcare Startup. Founded by a team of Ex-Max & Vedanta Doctors & Ex-UrbanClap, Mobikwik folks. We are disrupting the surgical ecosystem. In 5 years, we aim to be the largest distributed (full stack) surgery platform in India.

A team of 2000 + today with folks from pedigree backgrounds, we are looking to bring onboard teammates who can bring in the hustle and problem-solving attitude to help build Pristyn into a formidable company.

Pristyn Care is focused on building a healthcare ecosystem delivering surgical care, hospital admission, doctor's consultation, pre and post-surgical follow-ups and hassle-free insurance claims.

We built an asset-light healthcare ecosystem of 70+ clinics and 300+ partner hospitals across 30+ cities. The platform claims to have over 90 in-house super-specialty surgeons with expertise in general surgery, ENT, urology, gynecology, Opthal, vascular, and cosmetic surgeries

Procedures are performed using advanced laser and laparoscopic techniques; we have successfully performed 10,000+ surgeries till date.
