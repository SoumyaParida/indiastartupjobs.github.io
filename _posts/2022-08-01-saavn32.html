JOB TITLE: Senior Associate – Music Research

LOCATION: Mumbai, India

ABOUT US:

JioSaavn is South Asia’s leading music streaming service. We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favorite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

JioSaavn is looking for an enthusiastic and self-motivated music expert who can use their expertise to add rich meta tags to music on its platform. A successful intern will be someone who consumes a lot of music and has a keen interest in its various aspects such as the artists, genres, eras, moods, instrumentation and so on. So if you think you are obsessed with music, can work as part of a team, and are eager to learn about music you know nothing about, you could very well be one of our Music Researchers. We are looking at candidates for the following languages:

  • Kannada
  • English
  • Bhojpuri
  • Bengali

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Add rich meta tags to songs within the catalogue of the assigned language.
  • Ensure that the quality of tags added to songs is top notch.
  • Curate content for tag powered properties such as radio and hybrid playlists.
  • Track and review the performance of tag powered properties.
  • Contribute to team and organizational projects that are identified periodically.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Deep interest and knowledge of domestic and international music and films.
  • Musical knowledge in more than one language would be an advantage.
  • Knowledge of multiple Genres, Moods, Instrumentation and Categories of Music is a must.
  • Familiarity with the digital music space.
  • Strong Internet research skills.
  • Bachelor's degree in any stream.
  • Acute attention to detail.
  • Ability to multitask and meet deadlines.
  • Excellent communications skills.
  • Excellent listening skills, inquisitive, positive, team-oriented, enthusiastic approach.
  • Must be able to handle multiple projects in an organised manner.
  • You should know a lot (really a lot) about music..!
  • Overall a great personality.
