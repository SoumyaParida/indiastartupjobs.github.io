Job Responsibilities

This role is responsible for acting as a customer’s advocate within IT and play a neutral role. This is a massively impactful role and you'll have great opportunities to develop & foster various skill sets and expected to be an Individual contribute to a large extend. You will be responsible for driving the IT initiatives to various BU, understand the gaps between IT and respective business, bridge gaps if any,work with business on the business expansion plans and align the other IT functions towards the smooth support needs towards BU.

Below are some of the key responsibilities.

  • Build and maintain strong, long-lasting customer relationships
  • Develop trusted advisor relationships with customer stakeholders and executive sponsors.
  • Be an IT business partner for the BU (Work as the voice of customer ).
  • Engage with project and program management from each business unit to ensure they understand the objectives for joint strategic efforts.
  • Identify strategic business function opportunities, both short-term and long-term and track adoption across the relevant departments.
  • Identify business value when developing ideas with a focus on digital awareness, risk assessment, business continuity and business capability requirements.
  • Responsible for delivering Scope, Time, Cost KPIs.
  • Ensure the timely and successful delivery of solutions according to customer needs and objectives
  • Clearly communicate the progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives to internal and external stakeholders (Structured qualitative and quantitative review to be done with internal IT and stakeholders)
  • Forecast and track key metrics (expenses for month, delivery status, pending tasks with timeline, expansion plans, annual budget forecasts)
  • Analyse the data based on various matrices and generate meaningful reports.
  • Liaison with IT Projects, IT Infra ,IT Service delivery, Infosec and Tech to ensure the smooth support towards customers/BU function.
  • Drive possible automations towards the BU from IT and business perspective.
  • Convert business problem statements to technology related solutions.
  • Responsible for end to end process improvement including all KPIs. Focus on NPS, Cost reduction etc.
  • Drive implementing new IT initiatives towards customers/BU functions.
  • Maintain creativity and innovation by staying on top of industry and technological trends.
