In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

As an Associate Manager, Subscriptions-CRM in the Consumer Pay Team, you’ll play an integral part in the effective implementation of the JioSaavn Pro Global Subscription strategy with an aim of increasing subscriber base of JioSaavn Pro offering and be a market leader in music subscription business. The right person for this job should have excellent problem solving and analytical skills, strong cross-functional collaboration and stakeholder management experience, excellent time-management and organizational habits and the ability to clearly communicate and present information. You’ll join a fast-paced environment and cross-functional team, where you will assist in growth initiatives of a broad range and serve as an overall ambassador of the JioSaavn Pro brand. This role will report into Associate Director-Consumer Pay

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Data Mining & Interpretation: You will focus on data collection and interpretation from multiple data sources with a higher focus on what data to look for, to drive the next level of growth
  • Creating Dashboards, tracking & reporting: You will be responsible for creating dashboards for presenting data points on campaign insights to senior management and automate the processes for ready reporting
  • Growth Experiments & Execution: You will own a portfolio of growth experiments and support execution cross-functionally, including working with PMs, DS, and Engg on PRDs and leadership review, aligning other functions as needed, securing necessary approvals, driving launch planning, internal communication and rollout execution
  • Stakeholder Management: You will be required to work closely with Growth Managers to understand the goals and audience for every campaign and
  • Cost Benefit Analysis: You will be responsible for launching experiments while factoring in the impact on revenue; with an ability to do price sensitivity analysis and cost vs growth
  • Ownership: You will be responsible for owning analytically driven projects across business, marketing, operations
  • Opportunity identification & evaluation: in addition to coming up with your own ideas, partner closely with other functions to look at funnel drop-offs and changes, competitor funnels and product improvements, and input from partner teams (e.g., Ad ops, product, CRM, marketing teams) to build and maintain our prioritized pipeline of growth opportunities.
  • Maintain a structured and data-driven prioritization framework across opportunities.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelors in Engineering and MBA from Tier 1 colleges
  • 2-3 years of work experience in top-tier management consulting firm or growth analytics/Ops function in a tech startup
  • Excellent number crunching skills and quantitative aptitude to process and refine large set of raw data
  • Proficiency in Excel (Advanced), SQL, Hive and comfortable in creating dashboards on Excel, Google Sheets and Tableau
  • Proven track record of providing data driven insights to grow a business unit and leading it to execution with high ownership
  • Understanding of growth acquisition channels through new initiatives, discounts, product funnel optimisation, payment process optimisation
  • Excellent communication skills-verbal and written.
  • You can work cross-functionally with various types of teams and own outcomes
  • Razor-sharp attention to detail with an interest in defining and improving operational processes
  • Experience leading cross-functional stakeholders in order to execute high-impact projects
  • Flexibility to accommodate emerging responsibilities and last-minute changes
  • An ability to own and manage the execution of campaigns from front to back, including taking the initiative to troubleshoot errors, and assess and initiate improvements to the execution process
  • Available to join immediately or in 2-4 weeks
  • Ability to hustle and thrive in a young team of high performers
  • Experience in tech startup or top-tier strategy consulting, investment banking, or similar
