Pepper (www.peppercontent.in) is building India’s largest content marketplace that brings companies and content creators together to scale up content creation processes across 10 content verticals and 150+ content categories, 40+ content verticals, and 40+ global languages. Over the past 3 years, we’ve worked with brands like Adobe, upGrad, Swiggy, Dunzo, BusinessWorld, Digit, Shiksha.com, Rapido, BookMyShow, UrbanClap, Yatra, Bewakoof, YourStory, Bharat Matrimony, and 300+ other brands. We have over 30,000+ writers and graphic designers who have applied to Pepper so far, who form part of the ‘Pepper Cloud’.

We are on the path to building a global on-demand content marketplace platform that allows for collaboration, discovery and upskilling for all our creators. Now we are in a rapid expansion phase to enter into more content service offerings, engage a wide spectrum of content creators and at the same time, scale up our existing services.

We’re looking for someone who is enthusiastic, comfortable with uncertainty, flexible, great with people, understands our technical vision, and isn’t afraid to roll up their sleeves and get their hands dirty. Every day is an opportunity to bring your vision to life, and to expand, learn, and grow. No idea is left unconsidered. No voice is left unheard.

We are looking at getting someone to manage the language segment at Pepper and scale the vertical from scratch. This would involve setting up a network of creators, reviewers, and editorial individuals for all prominent Indian languages and global languages. This would involve strong interpersonal communication skills and the drive of ‘Getting work done!’

Segment-wise Descriptions of the role of :

Strategizing and launching new categories

  • Market research and understanding of the launch category
  • Make use cases of each understand the market size and competitors
  • Work with the product team for creating work-flows around the new category
  • Work with supply team to create ICP of the supply needed for the category and ensure supply targets are given and adhered too
  • Create the category launch & USP deck and train the sales team
  • Creating a feedback loop with the category ops team to close the feedback loop for each SKU launch issue(product, customer expectation, supply quality etc.)

Revenue & P&L management

  • Working with BD & account management teams on the sales funnel and creating full visibility of revenue both account-wise for the category
  • Ensuring the sales team is trained for the SKUs and understands the ideal ICP for each of the SKU. Thus formalizing a process of training every new joiner for the same (including deciding the bare threshold of understanding any sales rep has before pitching the product).
  • Working with the account management team to track retention cohorts across clients and work with the category ops team in getting retainer expectation setting right.
  • Working with the marketing team to keep a check on campaigns and lead throughput through the campaigns, thus inbound funnel tracking to be monitored.
  • Creating margin benchmarks for various SKUs and ensuring the category meets AOP margin targets

Category revenue analytics

  • Working with the analytics team and envisioning the right reporting structure
  • Own all pricing decisions for all subcategories through market analysis and system data
