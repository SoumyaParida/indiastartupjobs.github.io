About Swiggy, the team & role

We @Swiggy are on a mission to elevate the quality of life for urban consumers by offering unparalleled convenience. We are all about building beautiful products that make this world a fun, and a more convenient place to live in!

While Swiggy offers a multi-category experience, the Food Business continues to be the top of the chain generating close to 1.3 Million orders per day through a 2 lakh+ strong, independent fleet of Delivery Executives spread across 500 cities!

The Food Growth team is responsible for building products that aim at continued category Growth, elevated food buying experience and monetization for our food marketplace. The three major streams within food are

  • Food growth: Pricing, Promotions and New Service Offerings (Categories like Gourmet, Guiltfree)
  • Food Monetization: 1P Ads
  • Food storefront Products: Food discovery and buying experience

AVP of Product management at Swiggy will manage a high performance team of Senior Product Managers, create long term strategic vision for the food business, and build products to achieve this vision. She/he will partner with dedicated technology and business teams to deliver a high quality portfolio of products successful at scale.

She/he will work closely with business and technology teams to build a strategy and roadmap for Food, helping our users find value and convenience in the most efficient manner leading to continued customer delight and seamless experience.

This person will have a consistent track record of being a leader of leaders, thinking big and owning product life-cycle end to end (ideation to execution) and associated business impact. she/he will be a creative problem solver who can create high leverage products in a complex environment and is right a lot!
