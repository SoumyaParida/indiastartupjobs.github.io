Who we are:

Miko is a companion social robot for children in the age group of 4-10. Miko entertains, educates and provides a safe and trusted technological platform for children. We are present in over 80 countries and speak in more than 7 languages.

Utilities @ Miko:

As a young user, you can ask Miko questions about anything under the sun (including about Miko), have to-and-fro conversations about several intriguing topics, play games with Miko, ask Miko to tell stories or teach yoga, Dance with Miko and celebrate simple joys.

Utilities Huddle at Miko designs and develops tools to enable creation of user experience we want to provide using various modes of interactions Miko has. Utilities help internal team and external collaborators to Create, and manage contents for Miko, in a sense teach Miko how to do stuff. Utilities huddle engages in requirement gathering for various utilities from across teams. Some of the utilities include Expression creation, conversation curation utility, Talents management utility etc.

You will be part of this huddle, building various utilities for Miko 3 and upcoming Miko. As Miko keeps developing, so does the requirements for Utilities. So as a part of the huddle, you and the team would be constantly working with changing requirements and adding new or advance features to utilities.
