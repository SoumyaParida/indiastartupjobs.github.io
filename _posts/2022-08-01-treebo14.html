About the role:

The Regional Quality Controller (RQC) shall be the custodian of the brand-compliance and guest experience in their respective regions. The candidates shall be responsible:

  • Scrutinizing the audits conducted by the regional operations team to ensure highest standards of quality checks
  • Problem solving on improving guest experience by fixing recurring issues at the property
  • Act as a subject matter expert in the regional team on delivering great guest experience and train / coach the regional operations team as and when required
  • Represent the Treebo Quality Team in front of the hotel partners to drive adherence of the highest degree of compliance to the Treebo standards
  • Champion all Central Quality and Business initiatives in the region among the hotel partners and operations team
