Department

Operations

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Taking care of all the Export-Import and Logistics Activity including Customer Orders, Export Monthly Planning, Procurement Planning, Export Dispatch Planning through AIR/SEA.. Imports of Air / Sea Shipments. Bill Scrutiny, Monthly MIS. Etc
  • Monitoring overall Pre-Shipment and Post Shipment Documents.
  • Handling Container & Stuffing Plan for Export Shipments.
  • Liaising with Factory for Export dispatches and arranging for transportation.
  • Liaising with CHA for Export Clearance.
  • Liaising Export Post Shipment Documents with Bank under DA/DP/ADVANCE & LC documents.
  • Handling MEIS and Duty Drawback Scheme and DGFT issues.
  • Handling Hazardous Shipments.
  • Freight Quotes and Freight Negotiation with Forwarders for Sea & Air Shipments.
  • Handling Import Shipments both by Sea & Air.
  • Monitoring monthly Sales Booking under ERP system.
  • Maintaining monthly MIS Reports and Freight Provision.
  • Coordination with Production and Procurement Teams,
  • Handling Re-export and Re-import Shipments.
  • Preparation of Complete Pre-Shipment Documentation under L/C, DA, DP and Advance payment.
  • Preparation of Commercial Documents on receipt of BL / AWB as per payment Terms.
  • Negotiation of Documents with Bank.
  • Reducing overall lead time of material in shipping

  • Good Negotiation Skills

  • Manufacturing industry knowledge or background

Eligible candidates:

  • Desired Qualification: Diploma in Export Import Management /Any Graduation
  • Work Experience: 5-10 Years

We Value:

  • Constant learner to become a Subject Matter Expert
  • Proven record in start-up environment working with innovations
  • Exposure to a high paced working environment
  • Ability to conduct detailed procedures in a time-constrained environment
