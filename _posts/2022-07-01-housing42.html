What does this role hold for you…??

As a Senior Technical Support Engineer, you will be tasked with monitoring and coordinating with various stakeholders i.e., Sales team, Product team and Engineering team for addressing customers’ or business concerns and identifying respective solutions.

  • Taking ownership of customer and sales teams’ issues and seeing problems through to resolution
  • Researching, diagnosing, troubleshooting, and identifying solutions to resolve system issues
  • Following procedures for proper escalation of unresolved issues to the appropriate internal teams
  • Understanding of requirement specifications and absorb system knowledge in quick time
  • Liasoning between Engineering, Product and Sales teams for solving sales team and customer problems
  • Identify, record, document thoroughly and track bugs
  • Estimate, prioritize, plan and coordinate testing activities
  • Investigate the causes of non-conforming processes/functions and train users to adopt solutions

Apply if you have…

  • Expert level knowledge in Database and hands-on experience in developing SQLs with high level of complexities
  • An ability to multi-task and ability to address multiple teams simultaneously
  • An ability to prepare accurate and timely reports
  • Proven work experience of being a Technical Support engineer in an online product based organization
  • Familiarity with cross browser and cross platform application testing
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • An ability to provide step-by-step technical help, both written and verbal
  • Bachelors’ / Masters’ in Computer Science from a reputed institute.
