Role Summary

We are looking for an experienced Maintenance Manager to lead the team members in repairs and preventive / predictive maintenance on electrical and mechanical equipment primarily an EV vehicle. You will be involved in guiding your team members through an effective TAT on the R&M of vehicles.

Key Responsibilities:

  • You will be responsible for optimizing the Repairs and Maintenance (R&M) activity for an even better customer experience by increasing availability of well-maintained and usable bikes.
  • Ensuring all R&M activities on the field/road and the workshops are performed seamlessly.
  • Review and improve upon the various types of ongoing R&M activities across the city by conducting root cause analysis (RCA) and:
    • Optimization of the processes, methodologies and tools thereby reducing TAT and costs to conduct R&M per bike.
    • Improvements in the design and features for future versions of the bikes with the goal of ensuring availability to enhance overall customer experience while increasing asset (bike) utilization.
    • Ensuring availability of spare parts (OTIF) across all workshops/facilities in the city.
  • Setting up standardized workshop operations in all Yulu workstations, in line with the company best practices. Full responsibility of all the workshop operations and the staff, e.g. Mechanics etc.
  • Reviewing processes and implementing SOPs for on-ground implementation leading projects where necessary ensuring harmony with the five Yulu Operating Principles and the priorities.
  • Coordinating with the Central optimization and product teams for design and integration of R&M processes on Yuzen (Yulu operations app).
  • Keeping tabs on the latest developments in the city and spotting opportunities Yulu can leverage.
  • Hiring of R&M related staff, e.g. Mechanics, Yulu Pilots etc., addressing their training needs in coordination with the Learning and Development function, ensuring that all R&M staff abide by appropriate health and safety measures at all times.
  • Coordinate with other functional managers in the city and strengthen the profile of the R&M activity.
  • Identifying and raising various technical and other requirements to various central functions such as operations-design, technology, marketing, HR etc.
  • Using data to identify improvement areas, maintaining R&M dashboard and formulating plans to implement changes.
  • Reporting all R&M Activity status, progress and plans to city and functional heads.
  • Your performance will be measured on R&M cost (per: repair, bike, trip), availability of usable bikes, customer satisfaction index, employee happiness, the efficiency of the overall R&M activity operations.

Key Requirements:

  • 3+ years of experience in a similar role.
  • Understanding of the domain, i.e. R&M, operations, logistics, supply chain, fleet management and mobility. Experience in EV is preferred.
  • A go-getter with an execution-oriented mindset who is excited about making a big impact.
  • A data-driven decision-making approach.
  • Be a self-starter who thrives in a rapidly growing organization. Agility and ability to adapt quickly to changing requirements and scope and priorities.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skill

We assure you

  • A friendly culture where you are encouraged to share ideas and opinions.
