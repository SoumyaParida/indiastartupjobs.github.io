Job Responsibilities:

  • Explore the trillion dollar global potential of 3D and AR powered commerce.
  • Deeply understand what drives customer purchase decisions, what companies do today to meet those customer needs and how 3D/AR technology can address current pain points.
  • Ideate new formats for interactive 3D/AR commerce that deliver customer delight.
  • Design the UX for interactions between humans and 3D virtual objects and also between real and 3D virtual objects - today on smartphones and tomorrow on AR glasses.
  • Design experiences that leverage computer vision and voice recognition technologies for interaction.
  • Design the engine used to build these next level interactive shopping experiences.
  • Ride the massive Augmented Reality wave with the introduction of AR glasses.

Job Requirements

  • Have 6+ years of software design experience, preferably in e-commerce or retail.
  • Can solve complex design and human-computer interactivity problems using first principles thinking.
  • You have great clarity of thought and communication skills to translate these solutions for engineering teams to execute.
  • Exhibit exceptionally strong user empathy.
  • Are someone who loves learning and can step out of your comfort zone to quickly pick up and apply new technologies and concepts.
  • Preferably have experience working on e-commerce product description or brand pages.
  • Preferably have worked as a front-end developer before turning to design.
