We are a robot technology company building intralogistics solutions for the new age of warehousing. We incorporated in 2019 and are based out of Pune, India with a swiftly growing team of passionate minds.

Department

Operations

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Monitoring
    • Effective planning & execution of Preventive, Opportunity & Predictive maintenance of Robots and other equipment
    • Update Computerized Maintenance Management System database with PM activities, Equipment data and maintenance history
    • Monitors & evaluates equipment performance and initiates improvement activities.
    • Update working instructions for changed Equipment/processes.
  • Support
    • Conduct Root cause analysis of recurring problems and initiate corrective actions
    • Maintain high standards of OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) by providing support through Maintenance & Engineering solutions
    • Attends cross functional meetings and take responsibility for the assigned tasks
    • Impart training to stakeholders.
  • Cost Reduction & Value Optimization
    • Study & reduce maintenance costs, through optimum utilization of resources.
    • Encourage associates for initiation of Kaizen & support them for implementation.
    • Plan & carry out improvements / modifications of equipment to improve Productivity, Safety & Quality.
    • Reduce waste & improve process reliability by applying process improvement tools.
    • Identify & reduce non-value adding activities
  • Engineering Budget & Spares Management
    • Provide inputs for preparation of operating expenses budget
    • Estimation & planning of engineering spares, consumables & services
    • Coordinate with the central maintenance team for procurement of Engineering items. Inspection of incoming engineering items.
  • Environment, Occupational Safety, Health, Quality & Food Safety Management systems
    • Coordinates in setting individual and department objectives / metrics that contribute Plant Key Performance Indicators
    • Initiate Preventive actions to reduce potential risk in working area, related Environment, Occupational Safety, Health, Quality & Food Safety
    • Continuous monitoring of equipment and processes to ensure all Fire Safety & other EOSH Safety systems are in working condition.

Skills:

Service Parts Management; Budgeting; Maintenance Engineering; Maintenance Management Systems; Occupational Safety and Health; Automation or Conveyor systems Maintenance; Predictive Maintenance; Safety Management

  • Proficiency MS Office
  • Basic knowledge of Electronics systems like PCBs, Sensors and Mechanical tools
