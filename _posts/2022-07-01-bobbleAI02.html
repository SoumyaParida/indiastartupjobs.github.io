A graduate from Tier I & Tier II college preferably in Computer Science. Preference for Masters with a concentration in Data Sciences.

Required Experience:

At least 2+ years post Bachelors Or at least one internship post/during Masters in Data Science/AI Roles Or passionate people who have contributed to opensource projects/Kaggle/Github.

  • Write scripts to test for discrepancies in data for training.

  • Ability to write well documented, efficient, and readable code with Tensorflow, Numpy, Pandas, PySpark, Keras/Pytorch/Theano.
  • Mastery with one of MySQL, PostGreSQL databases and expertise with SQL queries.
  • Familiarity with some of the following python libraries: OpenCV, PIL, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Regex, Librosa, nltk, BeautifulSoup etc.
  • Ability to clearly understand and communicate key ideas and concepts, verbally, in tables/sheets, and written.
