Truein is a fast-growing B2B SaaS product company, offering Attendance & Timesheet solution to the companies with Contractual and Distributed workforce. 200+ customers across the globe now believe in what we do and have embarked on this journey with us.

Attendance tracking across multiple locations, Frequently changing staff, remote work sites, payroll-ready reports, and Workforce productivity – are some of the key problems faced by organizations across the globe.

At Truein, we are on a mission to bring transparency and controls in the attendance process. We leverage Face recognition and AI technology to create a seamless experience for both organization and their staff. We are backed by Investors and a high potential team.
