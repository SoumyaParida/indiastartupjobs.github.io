About the role

The legal head will manage all the legal aspects of Vivriti Asset Management. The role will ensure that company always operates within the law, offer counsel on legal issues, be an effective guardian of the organisation and ensure that the Organization’s interest and reputation is safeguarded at all times. The incumbent will also provide legal advice to management, work with internal and external stakeholders.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Provide expert and strategic legal advice to management. Deal with complex, significant matters that cut across legal and related areas
  • Act as a legal consultant and lead the debt transactions, setting up Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs)
  • Lead new investment transactions and manage the existing portfolio and taking enforcement actions for stressed assets or risk mitigating measures for potentially stressed assets
  • Provide general advisory and structure transactions, review and finalize documentation, participate in client negotiations
  • Advising and implementing governance standards in portfolio companies to ensure compliance with offshore and local laws and regulations
  • Working on complex transactions/issues that involves critical legal and regulatory analysis.
  • Provide accurate and timely counsel to executives in a variety of legal topics (labor law, partnerships, international ventures, corporate finance etc.
  • Set internal governance policies and manage the impact of external factors
  • Evaluate and weigh multiple inputs and impacts of any decision or course of action
  • Anticipate issues and estimate risks strategically- apply risk management strategies to mitigate legal and commercial risks.
  • Identify proactive solutions that will eliminate or mitigate risks
  • Create associations of trust and respect with key stakeholders, clients, investors
  • Deal with external parties (regulators, external counsel, politicians, clients and create relationships of trust.
  • Appropriately assess legal risk when business decisions are made, demonstrate particular consideration for the firm's reputation and safeguarding Vivriti, its clients and assets, by driving compliance with applicable laws, rules and regulations, adhering to policies, applying sound ethical judgment regarding personal behavior, conduct and business practices, and escalating, managing and reporting control issues with transparency, as well as effectively supervise the activity of others and create accountability with those who fail to maintain these standards.

  • Degree in Law. Proven general counselling experience in a business environment.

  • Overall broad legal knowledge (corporate law, litigations, labor law, etc), Ability to anticipate legal issues or risks.
  • High degree of professional ethics, integrity, and gravitas
  • Excellent judgement and analytical skills
  • Good interpersonal and communication skills
