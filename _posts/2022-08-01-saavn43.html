ABOUT US:

JioSaavn is South Asia’s leading music streaming service. We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favourite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

The Consumer Pay team at JioSaavn is seeking an Associate Program Manager to help manage and drive revenue projects involving both internal and external stakeholders. In this role, you will manage Partnerships, Ad Operations as well as coordinate with internal teams on various monetization projects. You will leverage your analytical, strategic thinking, and project management skills to help the Consumer Pay team prioritize and operationalize key revenue initiatives

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Work with external partners and internal stakeholders to help develop and implement JioSaavn Pro initiatives with an aim to increase subscribers and thereby revenue
  • Coordinate with various internal teams (Platform Product, Engineering, Legal, Finance, Content, and Marketing) to make sure timely go-live of Pro campaigns
  • Manage relationships with external partners to track performance and recommend ways to further optimize and improve performance
  • Track and analyze the performance of various Advertisements, and present data-driven learnings and recommendations
  • Help with market research to identify new monetization opportunities and communicate that to the team and senior leadership
  • Map competitor campaigns on a weekly basis and build learnings

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Degree in Business, Marketing, or related field
  • 2-3 years of work experience in Tech/Media/Entertainment group of a top-tier management consulting firm, and/or relevant experience at a B2C tech start-up
  • Effective in working autonomously to “get things done”
  • Strong people/ Stakeholder management. Ability to work collaboratively and build effective relationships across teams to solve and operationalize complex business initiatives
  • Experience in conducting and presenting quantitative and qualitative analyses and insights with simplicity, clarity, and confidence to various stakeholders including senior/executive management
  • Genuine, ambitious, open-minded, great conversationalist, positive attitude, and fun to be around
  • Understanding of growth acquisition channels
