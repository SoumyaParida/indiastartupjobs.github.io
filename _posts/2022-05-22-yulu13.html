Role Summary

We are looking for someone who is passionate about developing strategies based on insights from real people, and has the proficiency in their craft to turn these insights into appealingly designed products. The person we are looking for needs to be comfortable writing clear and concise content across multiple channels and touchpoints - including product names, labels, navigational elements, interface language, accessibility content, detailed UX guidelines around visual style, component behaviors, and editorial advice.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Bachelor's degree in English, Communications, Journalism, Technical Writing, UX, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience in user-focused digital product writing.
  • A web-based portfolio of relevant work samples with a UX focus; including problems you helped solve for, your contribution, and its impact.

Key Requirements:

  • 2+ years experience working on large, complex content strategies for UX.
  • Working knowledge of the language style guides, information architecture, nomenclature, and general UX principles.
  • Comfortable working across multiple simultaneous assignments and operating autonomously in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proven ability to present ideas and solutions to all levels within an organization, both verbally and in writing.
  • Ability to provide content solutions that work with product localisation
