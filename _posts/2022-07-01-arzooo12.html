Arzooo is where you belong!:

We're building E-commerce, Fintech & Supply Chain stacks for Indian Retail & heads down focused on solving the hard, industry-defining challenges. If you love the first principle approach, freedom to build & own your outcomes, Arzooo is the place! Welcoming all entrepreneurial minds to BUILD & blitz scale with us!!

We’re looking for a Technical Architect who is continuously adapting and excited to work on products that affect millions of people every day.

At Arzooo you will work cross-functionally to guide products from conception to launch by connecting the technical and business worlds.   • Manage the development of current and future features, user stories, and requirements while evaluating their potential and practicality by analysing customer needs.
  • Act as a leader within the company help drive product innovation into the corporate culture.

Our Ideal Candidate

  • An energetic, self-motivated individual focused on solving customer problems. She/He has a strong sense of product ownership coupled with a strong passion for technology and innovation.
  • Demonstrates curiosity and a growth mindset; fosters an environment that supports learning, innovation, and intelligent risk-taking; and exhibits resilience in the face of setbacks.
  • Relevant 4-10 years of experience working as a Product Manager preference for Graduates/MBA from ISB, IIMs, IIT or equivalent premier institutes & subject matter experts in Ecommerce / Logistics / Warehouse Management / Lending -Fintech stacks
