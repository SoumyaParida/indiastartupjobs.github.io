ROLE:

We are looking for a Manager/Senior Manager – Direct & Indirect Tax to be a key member of our growing Finance team. This role would report to the VP-Finance and will work closely with the internal and external customers to achieve the compliance, business, and financial goals of the organization.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Dealing and managing with notices, queries, investigations of various tax and statutory authorities, representing the Company in hearings in relation to tax matters, recording statements, etc on behalf of the Company
  • Proactively being connected with the recent developments in Direct and Indirect tax and undertaking impact assessment of any change in law, rules via notifications and circulars and disseminating the knowledge down to the Finance and Accounting team
  • Oversee and ensure timely preparation &; submission of direct taxes compliance reports, forms & returns of assigned areas.
  • Oversee and ensure compilation of monthly data, preparation of returns, and e-filing of corporate tax returns.
  • Compilation of TDS transaction & filing of TDS return.
  • Oversee and ensure compliance with all International and domestic transfer price regulations
  • Review and monitoring of transfer price audit reports ensuring they are in line with the business activities being conducted during the year and accordingly updating them as and when needed
  • Transfer Price strategy and risk mitigation
  • Representation before tax authorities for Transfer Price Assessments
  • Review of Business model and operations in line with recent/forthcoming changes in relation to tax treaties, BEPS, SEP rules, etc
  • Monitoring and Leading the tax reporting (in addition to normal Corporate and TDS returns) as and when required
  • Basic knowledge of US Taxes, filings, WHT rules, various forms and returns due on a periodic basis and working with the US Team and Tax Advisors to monitor ensure this is done on a timely basis
  • Any other responsibilities in relation to Direct and Indirect Tax matters as communicated by the Manager from time to time
  • Oversee all GST matters of the Company including preparation and filing of monthly GST returns, GST credit reconciliations, GST periodic audits, legacy GST matters, and queries and ensure timeliness and accuracy of the filings, troubleshooting escalated matters from Vendors/Partners/.Customers in relation to GST and arriving at the most favorable outcome for the Company
  • Independently assess the GST and other taxes impact on current and new projects and initiatives of the Business teams and advise them timely of the implications Oversee the preparation and filing of periodic GST refund claim, claim verification, and answering any queries from the tax office.
  • Lead Group (internal) and Statutory (external) GST audits and coordinate with internal teams for the details sought in the audits.
  • Work closely with Group Tax Advisors, consultants as and when required for ensuring timely responses to notices, tax litigations, etc
  • Handling service tax compliance refund claims and litigation for periods prior to GST implementation and all related legacy issues for which notices were received or not received.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • CA having tax (Direct and Indirect) experience with 6-8 years post qualification experience in Services Company (MNC/large private/public Limited Company)
  • Candidates with prior experience from the internet/online industry would be preferred (nice to have)
  • Ability to act and operate independently with minimal daily direction from manager to accomplish objectives
  • Expert knowledge of all Indian Direct and Indirect Tax Laws. Well versed with theknowledge of GST Act, Service Tax, Income Tax (including Transfer Pricing) Rules, Forms, Procedures, detailed theoretical knowledge of the Act and related Rules
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with an internal and external customer focus
  • Ability and willingness for hands-on working for Tally and any one ERP (must have)
  • Strong Excel skills (must have)
  • The candidate should be in touch with the latest developments on Direct and Indirect tax matters in India and worldwide (by reference to websites, journals, publications, etc) and should be able to communicate the recent changes to the Management.
  • Excellent communication skills with the capability to highlight critical issues with impact and provide solutions within and outside the Finance team
  • Strong analytical, decision-making, and problem-solving skills and abilities
