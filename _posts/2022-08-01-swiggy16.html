ABOUT THE TEAM AND THE ROLE

The Marketplace Fulfillment Platform (MFP) team consists of Swiggyâ€™s Core Logistics/Delivery, driver experience and CRM stacks. The charter of this team is to build a multi-tenant delivery platform that can seamlessly onboard multiple different businesses. The platform would need to maximize fleet efficiency across the various businesses. Additionally, the platform would allow third party Driver firms to onboard, while maintaining capacity across the various fleets, determine SLA promises and assignment across various multi-modal fleets and support pluggable inclusion and exclusion rules to allow matching of orders to drivers.

The team is responsible for creating multi-objective functions that optimize the efficiency of the fleet while providing a delightful Delivery experience for both the Customer and our Driver Partners, Demand supply matching using forecasting models at a hyperlocal level across 500+ cities and multiple business lines, optimizing on the sourcing strategy for hiring Driver Partners, seamless self service onboarding, training and tiering of our Driver Partners. The platform is also responsible for supporting multiple different Payout and Incentive constructs, including dynamic surges to mobilize driver partners in case of near term supply shortages, automated interventions to recover an order that may be getting delayed, and leveraging NLP to address customer queries in our chatbot.

What will you get to do here?

Senior Principal Software Engineers in Swiggy not only contribute to the high-level architecture of several systems but also contribute to the overall success of the product by driving technology and best practices in engineering in their respective organizations. They establish technology vision for respective teams and demonstrate how to solve a deeply complex and hard technical challenge, and help communicate that vision upward (CTO), inward (peers and engineering team) and outward (product & business teams). As a senior technical individual contributor you will be responsible for building and operating a scalable, high availability, multi-tenant, multi-fleet delivery stack to optimise for customer experience, while increasing the efficiency of the fleet.
