Job Description

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Manage product milestones, deployment cycles & overall product roadmap
  • Take ownership and drive ongoing improvements in the mobile app to be used by external customers
  • Scope and prioritize activities based on business and user impact
  • Keep abreast of the latest technological developments and continuously incorporate newer elements in product functionality after discussion with business teams
  • Manage technology, wire-framing, prototyping and other product tools
  • Ability to prioritize in an ambiguous environment. Generate new product ideas, enhancements to existing product using data analytics and customer feedback
  • Synthesize and drive product strategy from interactions with customers, business stakeholders, legal and policy teams, competitive insights, and market trends; build and manage an actionable product roadmap
  • Manage the entire product life cycle from strategic planning to go-to-market activities.
  • Balance trade-offs between time and scope while meeting user needs and business goals. Write detailed product requirements and end to end use cases with effective user stories to be used by engineering & design teams. Refine product backlog
  • Collaborate with product engineering and design teams to ensure alignment on roadmap and prioritization, and product delivery, sales and adoption. Work closely with our software engineers and architects on innovative solutions that take usability and scalability into account
  • Understand and champion customer needs. Collect customer feedback, nurture customer relationships, and bring the voice of the customer to your product development process
  • Be the subject matter expert internally on the product domain and understand and articulate the detailed end-to-end customer journey
  • Constantly assess your product-market fit, using customer insights, competitive analysis and your own industry knowledge to adapt to changing conditions and refine your approach
  • Engage cross-functionally with product marketing, sales, and other stakeholders to ensure flawless go-to-market execution of your roadmap
  • Produce content fo customers, engineering, sales, and product marketing to consume for product launches, training, or technical specs

Job Requirement

  • Exceptional leadership skills and proven experience in shaping team culture
  • 3 years plus experience in product management
  • Prior experience in handling a customer-facing product and scaling it
  • Preferring candidates from Tier-1 institute

ABOUT PRISTYN CARE:

Pristyn Care has raised around $100 million in its Series E round, led by Sequoia Capital US, valuing it at $1.4 billion, adding to India's Unicorn count.

Pristyn Care is an asset light full-stack healthcare provider that rents spare OT capacity and surgical infrastructure of a hospital to perform surgeries for its own patients via Pristyn's full-time surgeons. We do elective procedures in Proctology, Laparoscopy, ENT, Gynaecology, Obstetrics & IVF, Urology, Vascular, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, and Aesthetics. Pristyn Care has a team of high-quality surgeons, a tech-enabled patient acquisition engine, process-driven patient care, insurance clearance expertise and a network of Hospital partners with predictable OT availability. Founded by a team of ex-Max and Medanta doctors, and ex-UrbanClap and Mobikwik folk -Dr. Garima Sawhney, Dr. Vaibhav Kapoor and Harsimarbir Singh, we at Pristyn Care are disrupting the surgical ecosystem. In 5 years, we aim to be the largest distributed (full stack) surgery platform in India.

Pristyn Care, headquartered in Gurgaon, is operational since June 2018 and has since then expanded to 40+ cities across India. We are series E funded, led by Tiger Global, Epiq Capital. Sequoia Capital, Hummingbird Ventures and Greenoaks Capital are other key investors. With a team size of 1700+ people including 300+ surgeons, we are proud to have served more than 30,000 happy patients till now. Our surgeons are performing surgeries in 700+ hospitals pan India, including corporate hospitals. Pristyn care is among the esteemed list of official stakeholders with Swasth which has top 25 Indian startups along with best hospitals of India. Swasth's mission is to lay the foundation of a technology framework that will form a digital backbone for healthcare delivery in India (www.swasth.app). It aims to optimize hospital capacity by use of the latest digital technology.

Pristyn Care has also launched its own fitness and wellness brand named -beatXP. BeatXP is a wellness startup, founded in 2018 by Harsimarbir Singh, Dr Vaibhav Kapoor, and Dr Garima Sawhney, which was first launched with Pristyn Care brand name & then later on rebranded to beatXP in the year 2021.

beatXP aims to improve human lives with the help of technology and has introduced all necessary health monitoring devices such as BP Machine, Glucometer, weighing scale, thermometer, oximeter & many more to ensure all vital human body diagnostics cane be monitored easily anytime, anywhere from the comfort of their home. The Company believes in the motto “PREVENTION is better than CURE”, and human lives can easily be improved through good lifestyle & fitness habits. Company aims to launch premium quality fitness & wellness devices in the future with the sole aim to make human lives better & easier.
