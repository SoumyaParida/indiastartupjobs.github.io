About the role:

In this very critical role, you will be leading the voice sales team. This team plays a very important role in ensuring that all of our guests have a stellar voice support experience with us for their hotel accommodation requirements. You will be responsible for driving all relevant inputs and outcome metrics for this team.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Oversee overall operations for the team.
  • Drive all relevant input and outcome metrics for this team.
  • Utilize technology as an important driver to achieve the desired outcomes. Drive automation and self-serve for management of guest query and requests.
  • Keep the team motivated at all times.
  • Design, develop and deliver training modules, response templates and guide/coach the team on all aspects of written/verbal communication to meet the quality requirements for both process improvement and product introduction.
  • Coordinate across multiple functions to identify and fix bottlenecks that hamper efficiency and experience.
  • Own crisis management and ensure relevant closures are met efficiently.
  • Analyze optimum resource planning and maximize productivity.
  • Evaluate performance of the team by tracking key call center metrics.
  • Conduct regular workshops to empower team members such that they are equipped adequately to resolve guest queries and escalations.
  • Hire, train and onboard new folks and establish full floor readiness.
  • Be the voice of guests and provide feedback to the rest of the company.

Required Skills/Abilities:

  • Proven work experience as a contact center team lead or relevant role.
  • People management experience with ability to effectively manage, set team goals, develop people to their highest levels of performance, and manage through challenging managerial situations.
  • Analytical bent of mind
  • Stellar communication skills, both verbal and written, is a must
  • Ability to plan, multi-task and manage time effectively
  • Professional experience with optimization, processes, systematic organization, program/project management will be a big advantage.
  • Willingness to work in a 24*7 environment, learn, to innovate, to take initiative, to think beyond conventional dogma, ability to work in a flat organization.
