Job description

  • City Operations Manager will be responsible for the smooth functioning of all services that define customer experience at the residences.
  • City Operations Manager will also be responsible to build and train the ground operations team who will be the face of Stanza in front of students.
  • Own the P&L for all residences in the city and drive overall profitability by keeping a strong control over costs
  • Own NPS scores for the city and run projects to drive customer experience.
  • Hire Residence Captains and Micromarket Managers, and train them to manage the overall operations of each residence.
  • Oversee delivery of various services including food, hospitality, internet, laundry, and security to drive maximum customer experience
  • Build and grow best-in-class partner network across services; Monitor processes and improve service quality provided by different partners to ensure internal service level targets are met.
  • Define the day-to-day responsibilities of the team, set up reporting structure and monitor metrics on a regular basis.
  • Implement new tools created by the Technology team and drive usage.
  • Conduct onboarding and regular training of the field ops team

Required Candidate profile

  • Should have managed multiple outlets.
  • Should be ready to travel within the city.
