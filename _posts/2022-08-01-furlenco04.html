What is Furlenco?

Furlenco is India’s first subscription based furniture company. In an era of mobility, we find that a lot of people want the freedom to choose what they want their homes to look like without tying themselves down by buying furniture. Our business caters to the massive market of urban India.

What’s more, we have come through the COVID-19 pandemic situation unscathed! And we are growing. We are looking for exciting minds to join our tech team to drive further growth.

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor in Computer Science or related field
  • 6+ years of experience in software development and full product life-cycles
  • Experience in distributed systems and algorithms (Map-Reduce)
  • Experience with non-relational databases (MongoDb, Cassandra, Hbase, DynamoDb), designing schemas
  • Basic knowledge in machine learning, data mining, artificial intelligence, and statistics.

Preferred qualifications:

  • Coding skills in Java coupled with strong base in object-oriented design, functional programming and development
  • You understand basic data structures & algorithms, relational databases, RESTful web services and have good working knowledge of SQL
  • Experience on Java based web frameworks such as Spring Boot, Play, Dropwizard, or Java EE.
  • Experience in distributed system design and microservices. Exposure to messaging queues such as RabbitMQ, Apache Kafka
  • Excellent problem solving skills and understanding of Object Oriented Design and Patterns - GoF, anyone?
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to technically lead small to midsize teams and mentor junior members
  • Hands on experience with UI frameworks - React or Angular
  • Working experience with Docker/Kubernetes on AWS or any other cloud provider

Preferred qualifications:

  • You work on the core components of Furlenco technology stack - platforms powering our web portal, mobile apps, our customer experience systems, the warehouse & logistics management systems, billing & invoicing systems, payment infrastructure, risk & collection system, pricing engine etc. You will be part of a rewarding and challenging startup environment.
  • The role will allow you to lead a team and manage delivery of products & services from inception to delivery.
  • You will be both a highly technical hands-on engineer and an effective team leader. You will work with product managers to plan and execute releases, and realize the vision of our next generation products.
  • You will be hands-on with system design, data structures, relational and NoSQL databases, RESTful web services, Object Oriented Design and Patterns, handling scale
