Job Responsibilities:

  • Supervising operations of the Accounts Receivable (AR) section with focus on ensuring efficient processing (payment allocation, reconciliation and month-end reporting) of company receivables and timely collection in accordance to company policies.
  • Coordinating with customers and sales teams for the collection of overdue / long outstanding accounts.
  • Preparing reports and analyses on compliance and adverse trends along with appropriate recommendations or conclusions.
  • Reviewing the agreements/SO, getting discrepancies corrected if any, maintaining tracker for validation on the SO/agreements terms & conditions and manage customer database.
  • Checking the calculation of loan disbursal, interest, and getting discrepancies corrected immediately if any and verifying supporting for ensuring the correctness of invoices.
  • Sending weekly reminders & notices and taking follow-ups for recovery and manage the collection. Also, performing activities for quarterly as well as yearly balance confirmation.
  • Handling Loan Book / DPD etc.
  • Handling Securitisation/DA (Payouts Working etc.)
  • Allocating receipts as well as booking bank charges and performing with-holding tax reconciliation.
  • Produce of daily, monthly and weekly various MIS reports and ageing.
  • Calculating DSO, unbilled and deferred revenue and revenue for the month.
  • Filing of GSTR1 and reconciliation with 26AS. Also, accountable for Debtors reconciliation.
  • Gather and review supporting documentation for Internal and external audits.

Qualifications:

  • The candidate should be a graduate in Commerce/MBA and have proven working experience as Accounts Receivable.
  • Must carrying 2-4 years of experience in Lending/NBFC Firms.
  • The candidate must be high in attention to detail and meticulousness.
  • Comfortable in verbal and written communication
  • Advanced mathematical and logical deduction skills
  • Ability to learn quickly to be able to understand organizational processes and follow them.
  • Must be well organized and methodical in approach and be able to manage filing/database system.
  • Must understand the company policy documentation and how to apply them within day-to-day work and avoid leakages.
