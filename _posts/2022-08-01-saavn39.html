JOB TITLE: Manager - Strategy & Operations

LOCATION: MUMBAI, INDIA

ABOUT US:

JioSaavn is South Asia’s leading music streaming service. We are over 300 entrepreneurs, across New York, California, Mumbai, Gurgaon, and Bangalore, who help music lovers access, discover, and listen to their favourite songs across languages and genres. In early 2018, a merger between JioMusic and Saavn was announced; creating a combined entity valued at over $1 billion.

We blend digital technology, data analysis (which we have affectionately coined Music Science), and a strong, fearless business acumen to reach all corners of the globe. Through partnerships with Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Twitter and Shazam - to name a few - JioSaavn reaches more music fans across the world. Our award-winning mobile products, partnerships, innovations and thought leadership have been featured in some of the world’s leading publications, from The New York Times, to The Wall Street Journal, The Economic Times to Forbes, and many more. We are well-funded by some of the world’s most successful institutional investors and global media companies as well as a number of strategic individuals. Beyond investing, they are advisors and supporters of our vision, our passion, and our collective ability to deliver a revolutionary music experience as the leader in India.

ROLE:

The Strategy & Operations team is seeking a Sr.Manager/Manager to help manage and execute cross-departmental firm initiatives involving the Marketing, Product, Content, and Partnership teams. You are expected to leverage your analytical, strategic thinking, and project management skills to help teams prioritize and drive key firm initiatives forward.

The role is highly visible across the company and in particular to the senior leadership of JioSaavn. The Sr.Manager/Manager position will be based out of our Mumbai office.

RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Maintaining detailed project-related data dashboards and analyzing, and extracting key insights to help guide decision-making among cross-department teams
  • Modeling detailed project objective scenarios that help measure the impact of key initiatives
  • Support strategy development using quantitative and qualitative analysis, macro and internal data research
  • Developing and tracking project-specific KPIs for cross-departmental working groups
  • Presenting data-driven, insightful, and compelling recommendations on high priority strategic and operational initiatives
  • Driving execution of recommendations throughout JioSaavn’s fast-paced and dynamic environment
  • Staying up to speed on industry best practices as it pertains to the music streaming sector

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Degree in Business, Marketing, Finance, Engineering or related field
  • 6+ years experience in a top-tier management consulting firm or investment bank, and/or relevant experience at a tech company
  • Preferred experience in measuring the impact of marketing campaigns and/or product initiatives
  • Exceptional project management and stakeholder management skills
  • Exceptional proficiency in data analysis and modeling (SQL proficiency a plus)
  • Experience in conducting and presenting quantitative and qualitative analyses and insights with simplicity, clarity, and confidence to various stakeholders including senior/executive management
  • Exceptional proficiency in data analysis and financial modelling (SQL proficiency a plus)
  • Ability to work collaboratively and build effective relationships across teams to solve and operationalize complex business problems
  • An entrepreneurial, “roll up your sleeves” attitude and willingness to perform tasks with a range of difficulty from routine tasks to preparing analysis for senior management
  • Experience working with an international team
  • Authentic, intellect, open-minded, good listener, positive, and fun
