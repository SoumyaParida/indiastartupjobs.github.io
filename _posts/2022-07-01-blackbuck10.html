Key Job Purpose

Retention Manager will be responsible for improving the engagement of subscribed shippers and truckers in the BlackBuck platforms such that there is higher retention and renewals.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Analyse reasons for the low retention levels among different customer segments for the BlackBuck product
  • Solution for the identified reasons and execute measures to improve customer retention
  • Ideate on increasing product recall in the minds of shipper and truckers
  • Increase wallet share for both the customer bases resulting in higher engagement and retention
  • Drive subscription renewals for both shippers and truckers

Requisites / Prerequisites:

  • Minimum 2-3 years of prior experience in retention of customers / vendors
  • Exposure to managing tele-calling channel is preferred
