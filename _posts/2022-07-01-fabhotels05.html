Job Role:

Key responsibilities:

  • You will be responsible for guest experience on our web site, mobile web and Android/ iOS app
  • Ensure that the experience is seamless on various devices, as the same user may use the different device in different situations
  • Suggest, define and create wireframing/ mocks of improvements to our sites and apps experiences
  • Develop prototypes for user testing
  • Gather feedback and iterating over designs.
  • Work closely with developers to design the workflows.
  • Conduct frequent, iterative usability testing in parallel with the development.
  • Create and maintain a site-wide usability framework and guide.

Experience & Educational Qualifications :

  • 2-5 years of experience in UI/ UX implementation for consumer products, preferably in travel/ hotel space. Proven experience with the product development cycle of successfully launched web and mobile applications.
  • Understanding of and experience in corporate branding, layout, color theory and typography
  • The portfolio must exhibit an understanding of user experience design principles and knowledge of UI best practices and usability.
  • Skill in animation, video and/or story-boarding is a plus.
  • A passion for design with a strong opinion about aesthetics
  • A love for typography (using and choosing).
  • Experience designing mobile products from ideation to launch, for native iOS and Android.
  • Experience conducting user tests on usability and workflows.
  • Experience delivering sitemaps, wireframes, flow diagrams and other IA materials.
  • Knowledge of conversion metrics, and how they can help influence your designs.
