udaan is a B2B ecommerce platform that brings traders, wholesalers, distributors, retailers, and manufacturers in India on to a single platform. Our goal is to make ‘doing business’ easy in India & make B2B commerce convenient and efficient.

udaan Capital is the NBFC arm of Udaan & offers credit services to buyers and sellers on and off udaan’s B2B ecommerce platform to grow their business further. This space is largely untapped with less than 10% of MSMEs having access to working capital loans from financial institutions. In this way, udaan is uniquely positioned to formalize &scale trade credit.

Learn more about us: https://udaan.com/

Job Description

Function: udaan Capital

Sub Function: Risk Analytics

Location: Bangalore

Reports to: Risk Strategy Manager

Travel Frequency: 2 field visits per quarter

Department Hierarchy

Risk Analytics

Experience range

1 year - 3 years

Job Responsibilities

  • Develop and embed the processes, tools and policies needed to build robust credit strategies that are aligned to the business needs.
  • Define, monitor, and manage credit risk policies that take into consideration internal & external environments while supporting business growth in a fast-paced environment
  • Work with business teams to understand the nuances related to each category/industry, and provide actionable inputs to the modelling team and leverage models effectively as part of credit risk policies
  • Defining detailed analytic scope and methodology for risk assessment, validating hypothesis, and monitoring business lending credit policies

KRAs & KPIs of the role

  • Enable improvement of underwriting algorithms by introducing category level behaviors and external data sources which can help discriminate behavior better
  • Manage credit risk outcomes within agreed risk appetite thresholds
  • Support udaan Capital growth, while managing risk
  • Hiring and mentoring the team members to deliver the identified projects.

Functional Competencies

  • Analytical thinking and quantitative aptitude: Demonstrate Analytical thinking, problem-solving attitude, and a high level of comfort with numbers.
  • Ability to utilize statistical model to meet business objectives
  • Fetching and manipulating large data sets using SQL / Hive.
  • Analyzing and visualizing large data sets: R / python.
  • Decision making: Clearly articulate thinking around any problem and executes on decision making to deliver outcome.
  • Attention to detail: High attention to detail with commitment to accuracy & completeness.
  • Team and Stakeholder management: Ability to work successfully with multiple teams and stakeholders, manage disagreements maturely and align stakeholders. Ability to deliver outcomes through the team
  • Communication skills: Excellent Communication - both verbal and written.

Candidate Profile

  • Must hold an engineering/math/physics/ statistics degree from a top-tier school.
  • Must have 1-3 years of work experience, preferably in a fast-paced start-up or as an entrepreneur or an NBFC/FinTech or Financial or Analytics organization.
  • Experience working in the lending industry with demonstrable experience in credit risk policies within different banks / financial services.
  • Experience in underwriting using alternate data is preferred.
  • Experience in managing a small team
